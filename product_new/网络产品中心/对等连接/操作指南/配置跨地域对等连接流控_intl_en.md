1. Log in to [Tencent Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/) and choose **Products** > **Networking** > **Virtual Private Cloud** to access the Virtual Private Cloud (VPC) console.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Peering Connections** to go to the management page.
3. Click the ID of the target peering connection to go to its details page.
4. In the basic information section, click **Change Bandwidth**, and select the corresponding bandwidth.
 ![1](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/2e2192fe0bd390413fc122c340536d6a.png)
5. Click **Save**.
