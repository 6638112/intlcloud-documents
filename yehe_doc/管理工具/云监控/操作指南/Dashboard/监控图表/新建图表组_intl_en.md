This document describes how to create a chart group and associate and remove charts.

## Creating a Chart Group

1. Log in to the [Cloud Monitor console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/monitor).
2. In the left sidebar, click **Dashboard List** to go to the dashboard list page.
3. Find the dashboard for which you want to create charts, and click the dashboard name to go to the dashboard management page.
4. Click **<img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/827988040ba03fd73a5a95cc942eb5cd.png"  style="margin:0;" width="3%">** > **Create a Chart Group** to create a chart group. As shown in the figure below, you can rename or delete the chart group by hovering over the chart group and clicking the corresponding icon.
   ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/c68042acfad1dd428c7de30949e6a5f2.png)

## Associating a Chart with a Chart Group
Move a chart to the area below the chart group, click **<img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/84f5ba214a6bd401c4bc36fe20dd82a5.png"  style="margin:0;" width="3%">** > **<img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/717d61e61db001b9f2e9640041bd47c4.png"  style="margin:0;" width="3%">**, and confirm the association to finish associating the chart with the chart group.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/6248a3fca84edf872945b5d0dad8e35b.png)


## Removing a Chart from a Chart Group
Move a chart to the area above the chart group, click **<img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/68de185e07186165faab94bbca9e4f8d.png"  style="margin:0;" width="3%">**, and confirm the removal to finishing removing the chart from the chart group.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/b7500761b025bcef712c0a17447cc449.png)
