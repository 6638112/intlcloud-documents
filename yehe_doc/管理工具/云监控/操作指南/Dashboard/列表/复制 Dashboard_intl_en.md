This document describes how to copy a dashboard.

## Directions

1. Log in to the [Cloud Monitor console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/monitor).
2. In the left sidebar, click **Dashboard** > **Dashboard List** to go to the dashboard list page.
3. In the dashboard list, find the dashboard that you want to copy and click **Copy** under the “Operation” column.
4. In the window that appears, rename the dashboard and click **Save**.
