## Overview

After creating an organization, the organization creator cannot join other Tencent Cloud Organizations until this organization is deleted. The Tencent Cloud Organization information includes the organization ID, master account name, email address of the master account, and request control type.

## Creating an Organization

1. Go to [Tencent Cloud Organization](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and click **Organization Settings** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Create** to create an organization.


## Deleting an Organization

The creator of an organization can delete the Tencent Cloud Organization that they created.

1. Go to [Tencent Cloud Organization](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and click **Organization Settings** in the left sidebar.
2. Click the **Delete Organization** button in the upper-left corner. The **Delete Enterprise Organization** window appears.
3. Click **OK** to delete the organization.


## Quitting an Organization

An organization member can choose to quit the Tencent Cloud Organization they belong to.

1. Go to [Tencent Cloud Organization](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and click **Organization Settings** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Quit**. The **Quit Organization Confirmation** prompt appears.
3. Click **OK** to quit the organization.
