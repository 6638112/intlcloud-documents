## Overview

You (organization creator) can group organization members into units for easy management. The organizational structure allows for up to four levels of units.


## Creating Unit
1. Log in to the [TCO console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and click **Organizational Structure** on the left sidebar.
2. Click **Create Unit**. The **Create Unit** window will pop up.
3. Select the root unit name and enter the subunit name. Click **OK** to create the subunit under the root unit.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/5c87dd4b1b86ea3a6d76209763a0c343.png)

## Renaming Unit
1. Log in to the [TCO console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and click **Organizational Structure** on the left sidebar.
2. Click the edit icon on the right of the unit name. The **Edit unit name** window will pop up.
3. Enter the new unit name and click **OK**.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/5f09ca2c5c9eae2da8514332d5dc8b91.png)

## Deleting Unit
1. Log in to the [TCO console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and click **Organizational Structure** on the left sidebar.
2. Check the **checkbox** on the left of the unit name and click **Delete Unit** in the top left. The **Delete Unit** window will pop up.
3. Click **Delete**.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/4a2e46afa2bc17c48c317ba4e1bd4c98.png)

## Moving Member
A new member is placed under the root unit directory by default. You (organization creator) can move the member to the suitable unit.

1. Log in to the [TCO console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and click **Organizational Structure** on the left sidebar.
2. Check the **checkbox** on the left of the unit name and click **Move Member**. The **Move Member** window will pop up. You can check the **checkbox** next to **Name** to select all members.
3. Select the target unit and click **OK**.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/a01e70736d509b4588c8656f4ad42396.png)
