To help you monitor services in TMP, preset Grafana dashboards will be continuously optimized or added, and you can also update the preset Grafana dashboards.

## Directions

1. Log in to the [TMP console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/monitor/prometheus).
2. In the instance list, select the TMP instance to be upgraded and click **More** > **Grafana** > **Upgrade Dashboard** on the right.
3. In the **Upgrade Dashboard** window that pops up, click **OK**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/50593e8a4649109a5e152a92b62e144c.png)
