This document describes how to resume a test task, after which CAT will resume billing.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [CAT console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cat/probe/tasklist).
2. On the left sidebar, click **Tasks**.

### Resuming a test task
In the test task list, click **More** > **Resume** in the **Operation** column to resume a task.
![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/iBQJ182_20intl_%E5%A5%BD%E5%8E%8B%E7%9C%8B%E5%9B%BE.png)

### Batch resuming test tasks
Select multiple tasks and click **Resume** in the top-left corner to resume them.
![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/c4QS435_21intl_%E5%A5%BD%E5%8E%8B%E7%9C%8B%E5%9B%BE.png)
