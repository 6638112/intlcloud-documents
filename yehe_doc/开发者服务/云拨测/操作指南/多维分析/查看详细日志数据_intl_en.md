This document describes how to view the detailed log data of a test task.

## Directions

1. Log in to the [CAT console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cat/probe/tasklist).
2. Click **Test Statistics** on the left sidebar and select a test task.
3. Scroll down to the detailed data list and click any column to view the log data of the test task.
