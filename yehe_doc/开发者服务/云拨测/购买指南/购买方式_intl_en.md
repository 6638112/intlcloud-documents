This document describes how to purchase the CAT service in the console.

## Directions

### Free edition
1. Go to [**Tasks**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cat/probe/tasklist) in the CAT console.
2. Click **Create task**, configure parameters as needed, and click **Create**. You will have the Free edition after creating a task successfully during your first use of CAT.
![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/8TtK406_41intl_%E5%A5%BD%E5%8E%8B%E7%9C%8B%E5%9B%BE.png)
>? The free trial period is 15 days.

### Pro edition
1. Go to [**Create task**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cat/probe/tasklist) in the CAT console.
2. Move the cursor to **Free edition** in the top-right corner and click **Upgrade to Pro edition** in the box.
3. In the pop-up window, select **Service Level Agreement** and click **OK**.
