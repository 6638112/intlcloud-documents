The evaluation of enabled conformance packs in Config can be automatically triggered by the system or manually triggered by users:
1. Manual trigger
Create or edit a conformance pack.
Then, go to the **Conformance pack details** page and click **Evaluate** to evaluate the conformance pack.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/14fc3ae283a617b326b1a620719cadd1.png)
2. Automatic trigger
Configure a frequency in a rule of a conformance pack. Then, the system will automatically evaluate the conformance pack according to the frequency.
