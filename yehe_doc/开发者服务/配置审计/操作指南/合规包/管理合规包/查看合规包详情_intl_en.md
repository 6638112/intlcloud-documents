On the **Conformance pack list** page, click the name of a conformance pack to view its details. The corresponding **Conformance pack details** page displays the basics, included rules and rule evaluation results of the conformance pack. On this page, you can **edit** or **delete** the conformance pack, trigger evaluation of the conformance pack, or **add, remove, or delete** rules.
