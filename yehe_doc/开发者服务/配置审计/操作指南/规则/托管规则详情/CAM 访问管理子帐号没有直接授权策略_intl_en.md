**Description:** Checks whether there are direct authorization policies under a CAM sub-user.
**Compliance check logic:** If no authorization policy is directly added for the current CAM sub-user, this rule is compliant.
**Rule ID:** `cam-user-policy-directly-bound`
**Risk level:** Low
**Supported resource types:** `QCS::CAM::User`
**Trigger type:** Periodic, 24 hours
**Keywords:** User, policy
**Input parameters:** None
