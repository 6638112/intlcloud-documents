## Global resource monitoring
Discover and view configurations of your resources scattered in different regions under your account in a centralized way, enabling you to get a holistic picture of your resources.
## Fast review of change histories
Continuously record and save configuration changes and compliance evaluations of each resource, allowing you to review the operation history at any time and identify root causes.
## Resource governance and compliant Ops
Leverage managed rules and conformance packs based on Tencent Cloud's best practices for continuous resource evaluations, helping you govern cloud resources and regulate cloud Ops timely.
