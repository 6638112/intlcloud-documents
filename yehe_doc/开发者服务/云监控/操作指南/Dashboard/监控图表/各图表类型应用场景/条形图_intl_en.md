This document describes the use cases and operations directions of bar charts.

## Use Cases

**Bar chart**: is applicable to the comparison between metrics in each statistical period.

**Configuration effects of bar charts:**
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/0f8f8550c9c6a35d17619714f10e3471.png)

## Directions

1. Log in to the Cloud Monitor console and click **Dashboard** > **[Default Dashboard](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/monitor/dashboard2/default)** on the left sidebar.
2. Click **<img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/09d4ca5824542316bf485350e4d5f62f.png" width="3%"></img>** > **Create a Chart** to go to the chart editing page.
3. In the chart configuration section, select “Bar Chart” as the chart type.
4. (Optional) Under “Chart Elements,” you can specify whether to display units. For more information on the meanings of units, see [List of Units](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/248/38478).
5. After the configuration is completed, click **Save** in the upper-right corner.
