
This document describes how to enable the dark mode, which uses the optimal contrast for charts, text foreground, and background. It highlights the content consistency, comfort, and legibility compared to the light mode.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [Tencent Cloud console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com).
2. Hover over the personal account icon in the top-right corner and click **Dark** in the **Preferences** settings from the drop-down list.
