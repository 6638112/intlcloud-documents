This document describes how to view APM alarm records.

## Viewing alarm records
1. Log in to the CM console and go to the [**Alarm Records**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/monitor/alarm2/history) page.
2. Click **Advanced Filter**, select **APM** as the monitoring type, and click **Query**.
3. You can also set a time range in the top-left corner to filter alarm records during this period.
