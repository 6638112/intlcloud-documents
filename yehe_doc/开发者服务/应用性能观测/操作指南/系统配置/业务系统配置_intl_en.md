You can modify the basic information of the target business system on the **System configuration** > **Business system configuration** page.


## Prerequisites
1. Go to the [**System management**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/apm/monitor/settings) page in the APM console.
2. Go to **Business system configuration**.

### Modifying basic information
At the top of the page, select the target business system and click **Edit** on the right of **Basic info**. You can modify the retention period, business system name, and tag.
