## Overview
An organization creator can view and edit member information, delete and invite organization members, and perform other related operations in member management. Member information includes name, notes, account ID, and joining time. The name and notes are editable while the account ID and joining time are not.

## Viewing and Editing the Member List

1. Log in to the [TCO Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and select **Member Management** > **Member List** on the left sidebar to view the member information of the current organization.
2. Click the **Edit** icon next to the member name to rename the member. Enter the new member name in the edit box that pops up and click **OK** to save the change. Member notes can be modified in the same way.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/26099f9fbf4752689b4ea2ee4e6d55ec.png)

## Deleting an Organization Member

>!
>- A deleted organization member cannot be viewed or edited in the member list and cannot be displayed or moved in the organization structure.
>- The account of the organization creator cannot be deleted.

1. Log in to the [TCO Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and select **Member Management** > **Member List** on the left sidebar.
2. Check the **checkbox** to the left of a member name to select the member to be deleted. Check the **checkbox** next to "Name" to select all members.
3. Click **Delete Member** above the member list to delete the selected members.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/050318b2c79f684ae537076a12876445.png)

## Inviting an Organization Member

An organization creator can invite new members to join the organization.

1. Log in to the [TCO Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and select **Member Management** > **Member List** on the left sidebar.
2. Click **Invite Member** in the top-left corner. An **Invite Members** window will pop up. Enter the member account ID, name, and notes, then click **Invite** to send the invitation. The member account ID and name are required, while notes are optional.

>? You can also invite members by navigating to **Member Management** > **Invitations Sent** on the left sidebar and clicking **Invite Member** in the top-left corner.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/25806472fa0da53ef1a44dfa49597876.png)

## Canceling an Invitation

An organization creator can cancel an invitation if it has not been accepted by the invitee.

1. Log in to the [TCO Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and select **Member Management** > **Invitations Sent** on the left sidebar.
2. Click **Cancel Invitation** next to the target invitee in invitations sent. In the pop-up window, click **OK**.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/20e7a9a12189218f2ddc5e23e71ff012.png)

## Accepting an Invitation

An organization member can accept or reject an invitation from the organization creator. Organization members can join only one organization at a time. In received invitations, members can view the organization information of the inviter, which includes the organization ID, root account name, root account email, requested control type, status, and operations.

1. Log in to the [TCO Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization) and select **Member Management** > **Invitations Received** on the left sidebar.
2. Click **Accept** to join the organization or click **Reject** to reject the invitation.
