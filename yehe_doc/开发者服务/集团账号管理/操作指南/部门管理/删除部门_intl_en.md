You can delete an organization in the TCO console when you no longer need it.

## Directions[](id:deleteUnit)
1. Log in to the TCO console and select **[Organization structure](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization/relation)** on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Organization structure** page, select the target department and click **Delete department** above.
![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/eKps279_%E5%88%A0%E9%99%A4%E9%83%A8%E9%97%A8.png)
3. In the **Delete department** pop-up window, click **Delete**.
