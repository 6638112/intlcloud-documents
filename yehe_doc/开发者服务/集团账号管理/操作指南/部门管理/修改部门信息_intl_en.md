This document describes how to modify the department information in the TCO console.

## Directions[](id:modifyUnit)
Log in to the TCO console, select **[Department management](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization/relation)** on the left sidebar, and modify the department information as needed.


### Renaming a department
Click <img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/4eba4d9442ce077d5ea68c12304fed08.png" style="margin:-3px 0px"> on the right of the department name. In the pop-up **Edit department name** window, enter a new name and click **OK**.
![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/D8Yr220_%E4%BF%AE%E6%94%B9%E9%83%A8%E9%97%A8%E5%90%8D%E7%A7%B0.png)

### Modifying the department description or tag
In the window on the right of the **Organizational structure** page, click <img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/4eba4d9442ce077d5ea68c12304fed08.png" style="margin:-3px 0px"> on the right of the description or tag. In the pop-up window, edit the content and click **OK**.
![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/LUxL514_%E4%BF%AE%E6%94%B9%E9%83%A8%E9%97%A8%E6%8F%8F%E8%BF%B0%E5%8F%8A%E6%A0%87%E7%AD%BE.png)
