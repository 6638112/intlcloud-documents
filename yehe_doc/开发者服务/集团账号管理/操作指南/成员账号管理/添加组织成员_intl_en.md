The organization creator can add a member to an organization through invitation or creation.

## Directions[](id:addMembers)
Choose a method of adding a member as needed:
<dx-tabs>
::: Inviting a member[](id:inviteMembers)
1. Log in to the TCO console and click **[Member account management](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization/member)** on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Member list** page, click **Add member**.
3. On the **Add member** page, use the default option **Invite member**.
![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/vAcr842_%E9%82%80%E8%AF%B7%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87%E6%88%90%E5%91%98.png)
4. Set the account ID, member name, financial permission, payment mode, department, and whether to allow the member to quit the organization. You can get the account ID on the [Account information](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/developer) page.
5. After completing the configuration, click **OK**, and the information of the invitee will be verified. The invitee can be invited successfully only if the invitee account ID has completed enterprise identity verification, the verified entity is the same as the invitee, and the invitee hasn't joined any organizations.
The successful invitation is valid for 15 days. You can select **[Organization change record](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization/invitations)** on the left sidebar and click the **Member invitation record** tab to view the invitation information.
:::

::: Creating a member[](id:newMember)

1. Log in to the TCO console and click **[Member account management](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization/member)** on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Member list** page, click **Add member**.
![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/ypAF602_%E6%96%B0%E5%BB%BA%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87%E6%88%90%E5%91%98.png)
3. Set the name, access permission, financial permission, payment mode, and department.
 - You can create a permission as instructed in [Managing Access Permission]().
 - You can create a department as instructed in [Creating Department](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1031/51858).
4. Click **OK**, and the member account will be created automatically and inherit the identity information of the creator.
You can select **[Organization change record](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization/invitations)** on the left sidebar and click **Member change record** > **Member creation record** to view the creation record and result.


:::
</dx-tabs>
