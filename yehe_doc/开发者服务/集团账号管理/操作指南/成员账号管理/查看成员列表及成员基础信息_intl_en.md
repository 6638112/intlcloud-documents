This document describes how to view the member list and basic member information in the TCO console.

## Directions[](id:viewMemberList)
1. Log in to the TCO console and select **[Member account management](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization/member)** on the left sidebar to view the information of members in the current organization.
The member information includes the member name, member account ID, member `APPID`, join method, access permission, financial permission, payment mode, department, join time, and whether the member is allowed to quit the organization. The member name, financial permission, and department can be modified.
2. On the **Member list** page, click <img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/4eba4d9442ce077d5ea68c12304fed08.png" style="margin:-3px 0px"> on the row of the target member.
3. In the pop-up input box, enter the new member name and click **OK**.
![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/WIQb085_%E6%9F%A5%E7%9C%8B%E6%88%90%E5%91%98%E5%88%97%E8%A1%A8%E5%8F%8A%E6%88%90%E5%91%98%E5%9F%BA%E7%A1%80%E4%BF%A1%E6%81%AF.png)
4. Click **Edit** on the right of the target member. In the **Edit member** pop-up window, select the target department in the **Department** drop-down list and click **OK**.
![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/daNt331_%E4%BC%81%E4%B8%9A%E5%BE%AE%E4%BF%A1%E6%88%AA%E5%9B%BE_20221226180618.png)
