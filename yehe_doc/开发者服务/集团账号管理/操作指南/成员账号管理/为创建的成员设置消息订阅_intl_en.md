## Overview

By default, members created in the TCO console do not have contact information and therefore cannot receive notification messages via SMS or email. This document describes how to configure message subscription for created members.

## Directions

1. Log in to the TCO console as instructed in [Granting Member Account Access](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1031/51867).

2. Log in to the CAM console and select **Users** > [User List](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cam) on the left sidebar.

3. On the **User List** page, click a username to enter the user details page.

4. Click the ![img](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/image/document/4689d3349f21e8253058793d9ed8d5f9.png) icon next to each contact method and add the mobile number and email for the member as prompted. ![img](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/iEIN348_%E6%88%AA%E5%B1%8F2022-12-28%2017.14.19.png)

5. After that, click **Subscribe to Messages** in the **Quick Action** module on the right. ![img](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/yehe/backend-news/nCYI626_%E6%88%AA%E5%B1%8F2022-12-28%2017.14.46.png)

6. In the pop-up window, select the message types as needed.

>?You can also create a sub-user under the member account and receive messages via the sub-user as instructed above.
