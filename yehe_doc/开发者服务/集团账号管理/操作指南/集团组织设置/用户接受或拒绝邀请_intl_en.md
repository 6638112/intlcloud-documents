You can accept or reject invitations from organizations in the TCO console.

## Directions
Log in to the TCO console and click **[Basic information](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization/setting)** on the left sidebar. On the **Basic information** page, you can view valid invitation records and click **OK** to join an organization.
To refuse to join the organization, click **Reject**.


<dx-alert infotype="explain" title="">
- Only users that completed enterprise identity verification can join an organization. For more information, see [Enterprise Identity Verification Guide](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/378/10496).
- Your verified entity must be the same as the invitee or has been added to the organization's verified entity information.
- After you join the organization, the invitation list will be hidden until you quit the organization.
</dx-alert>
