If you complete enterprise identity verification and haven't joined or created an organization, you can create one in the TCO console.

## Directions[](id:createOrganization)
Log in to the TCO console, click **[Basic information](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/organization/setting)** on the left sidebar, and click **Create** to create an organization.
<dx-alert infotype="explain" title="">

- Only users that complete enterprise identity verification can create an organization. For more information, see [Enterprise Identity Verification Guide](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/378/10496).
- After creating the organization successfully, you cannot join another organization until the created organization is deleted.
</dx-alert>
