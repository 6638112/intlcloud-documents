### Who can view the iteration list?
All project members can view the list.

[](id:que2)
### How are iterations sorted?
Iterations are sorted by successive phases: not started, in progress, and completed. Iterations cannot be reverted to the previous phase.

[](id:que3)
### What are the issue statuses?
The three basic issue statuses are: not started, in progress, and completed.

[](id:que4)
### Can I add a sub-requirement to an iteration?
Sub-requirements cannot be added to iterations separately. Requirement descriptions of and statistics in iterations refer to level-1 requirements and do not include sub-requirements.

[](id:que5)
### What are the default requirement statuses?
By default, requirements have five statuses: pending review, reviewed, developing, released, and closed.
