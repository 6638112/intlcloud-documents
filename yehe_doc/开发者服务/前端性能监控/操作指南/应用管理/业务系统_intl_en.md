This document describes how to use the business system feature. You can use business systems to manage your connected applications by group.

## Directions

### Creating business system

1. Log in to the [RUM console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/rum).
2. On the left sidebar, click **Application Management** > **Business System**.
3. On the business system page, click **Create Business System**.
4. In the pop-up window, enter the business name and indicate your consent to the agreement.

### Editing business system
You can click **Edit** in the **Operation** column to edit the business system name and description.

## Tag

A tag is used to manage and control whether a sub-account user can access a business system connected to RUM. For detailed directions, see [Resource Tag](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1131/44513).
