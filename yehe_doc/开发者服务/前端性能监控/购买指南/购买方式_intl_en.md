After you create a business system to connect your application and your application reports a certain amount of data, RUM will start billing your usage based on the [billing rules](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1131/44490).


## Purchase Method

### Creating business system
1. Log in to the [RUM console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/rum).
2. On the left sidebar, click **Application Management** > **Business System**.
3. On the business system management page, click **Create Business System**. In the pop-up window, enter the business name and indicate your consent to the agreement.
<img src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/70bcf276ee3313233dfdfabb499d91d0.png"  height="60%" width="80%"> 


### Connecting application

Connect your application as instructed in [Application Connection](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1131/44496). After the connection succeeds and a certain amount of data is reported, billing will start.
