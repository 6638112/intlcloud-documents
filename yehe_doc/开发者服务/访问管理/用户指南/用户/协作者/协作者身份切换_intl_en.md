## Overview
This document describes how to switch the identity of the root account to which a collaborator belongs to manage the resources under the corresponding root account within the scope of permissions.
## Directions
1. Log in to the [Tencent Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com) and move the cursor to the account name in the top-right corner of the page.
2. In the drop-down menu that appears, click **Switch user identity** as shown below:
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/2d78fbef986a949b5ff314f3b4e8012e.png)
3. On the user identity selection page, click **âˆ¨** on the right of the account information. Select the root account identity that needs to be managed as shown below:
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/0d9844414cf232a96784a816bc8e901f.png)
4. Click **Sign in** to switch identity.
