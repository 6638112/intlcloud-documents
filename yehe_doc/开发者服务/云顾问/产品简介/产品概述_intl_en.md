Tencent Smart Advisor is an out-of-the-box product that assesses risks for Tencent Cloud resources. After Tencent Smart Advisor is granted to a [CAM service role](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/598/19421), it can quickly assess and analyze risks in cloud resources, application architecture, business performance, and security and then offer optimization suggestions online according to the actual business usage, helping improve the system security, business stability, and service reliability.

## List of supported products

Tencent Smart Advisor provides a wide variety of assessment items, flexible assessment configurations, and system optimization suggestions to help you improve business continuity.
It offers various risk assessment items in multiple dimensions, such as security, reliability, cost, service restriction, and performance for different Tencent Cloud products. Currently, it can conduct assessment in the following Tencent Cloud products:
