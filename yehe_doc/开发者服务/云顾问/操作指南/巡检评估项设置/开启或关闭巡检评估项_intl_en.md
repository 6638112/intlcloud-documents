## Overview

This document describes how to enable and disable assessment items in the Tencent Smart Advisor console. A disabled item will be excluded from the next inspection.



## Directions
1. Log in to the Tencent Smart Advisor console and select [**Assessment Items**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/advisor/switch) on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Assessment Settings** page, toggle on or off the **Switch** to enable or disable an assessment item.
