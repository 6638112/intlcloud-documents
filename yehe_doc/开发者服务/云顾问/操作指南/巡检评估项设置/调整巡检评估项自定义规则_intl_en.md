## Overview

You can customize rules for some assessment items for CVM, CBS, TencentDB, etc. as needed. This document describes how to modify risk assessment rules in the Tencent Smart Advisor console.



## Directions
1. Log in to the Tencent Smart Advisor console and select [**Assessment Items**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/advisor/switch) on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Assessment Items** page, click **Modify rules** on the right of the target item.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/3e593c17ce224f08a8224727f1054ce2.png)
3. In the **Modify rules** pop-up window, modify the risk trigger condition as needed.
4. Click **Confirm**.
