## Overview
This document describes how to query risk trends in the past 30 days in the Tencent Smart Advisor console.

## Directions
1. Log in to the Tencent Smart Advisor console and select [**Risks**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/advisor/history) on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Risks** page, select the dimension (product, risk level, or risk type) for trend analysis and the time range and then click **Search**.
