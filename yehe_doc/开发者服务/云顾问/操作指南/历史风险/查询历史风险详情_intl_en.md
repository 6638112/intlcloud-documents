## Overview
This document describes how to query risk details in the past 30 days in the Tencent Smart Advisor console.

## Directions
1. Log in to the Tencent Smart Advisor console and select [**Risks**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/advisor/history) on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Risk Details** tab, select the product, risk level, and time range and then click **Search**.
