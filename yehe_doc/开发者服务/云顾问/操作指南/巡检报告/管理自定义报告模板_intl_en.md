## Overview

When creating a subscription report, you can use the default or custom template. This document describes how to custom a report template in the dimension of product, risk type, or tag and modify or delete the report in the Tencent Smart Advisor console.


## Directions

### Creating a template
1. Log in to the Tencent Smart Advisor console and select [**Email Notification**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/advisor/subscribe) on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Report Templates** tab, click **Create**.
3. In the **Create Template** pop-up window, enter the **Template Name** and select the **Product**, **Risk Type**, and **Tag**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/6e127e32e3306459d373e5732cd62f4e.png)
4. Click **Create**.


### Modifying a template
1. On the [**Email Notification**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/advisor/subscribe) page, click **Edit** on the right of the target template.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/bb0c2a449505737b59a265703437a624.png)
2. In the **Edit Template** pop-up window, edit the information and click **OK**.


### Deleting a template
1. On the [**Email Notification**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/advisor/subscribe) page, click **Delete** on the right of the target template.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/2be30b85baf8e4b97783d03d28f55c69.png)
2. In the pop-up window, click **Delete**.
