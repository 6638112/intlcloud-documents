## Overview
Tencent Smart Advisor provides report subscription feature to email users their inspection reports. This document describes how to manage your subscriptions in the console, including creating, enabling/disabling, modifying, and deleting a subscription.


## Directions

### Creating a subscription
1. Log in to the Tencent Smart Advisor console and select [**Email Notification**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/advisor/subscribe) on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Subscriptions** tab, click **Create**.
3. In the **Create Subscription** pop-up window, enter the **Subscription Name**, toggle on **Enable**, and set the **Frequency**, **Time**, **Report Templates**, and **Email**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/860f070144db3f8af8e6a9865d2f835c.png)
4. Click **Create**.


### Enabling/Disabling a subscription
On the [**Subscriptions**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/advisor/subscribe) page, you can toggle on the **Enable/Disable** switch to enable/disable a subscription.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/d0a9b303c7f87962004984c96beb225e.png)


### Modifying a subscription
1. On the [**Subscriptions**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/advisor/subscribe) page, click **Edit** on the right of the target subscription.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/4e4b66d933d910ccae9b4c9389d34fa5.png)
2. In the **Edit Subscription** pop-up window, edit the parameters.
3. Click **OK**.


### Deleting a subscription
1. On the [**Subscriptions**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/advisor/subscribe) page, click **Delete** on the right of the target subscription.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/9dd866830a6bc9ebaeb45291529b3b9c.png)
2. In the pop-up window, click **Delete**.
