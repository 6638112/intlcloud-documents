As a reseller, you can query all your customers under your account and view their basic information and available credit.
Step 1. Log in to [Tencent Cloud](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/login) with your reseller account and enter the [Partner Center](https://console.intl.cloud.tencent.com/partners/customer/manage).
Step 2. Select **Customer Management** on the left sidebar.
Step 3. Manage your customers.
### 1. Querying Customer
You can query a customer by its account ID, name, email, or remarks.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/60f57adcb9bb68ec69b67005e60f0db2.png)
### 2. Exporting Customer
You can export the list of all your customers.
