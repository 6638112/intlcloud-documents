>1. As a reseller, you can only bind the newly registered customers.
>2. Currently, you cannot unbind customers online. You can contact your channel manager to unbind them offline if you need to.

### 1. Inviting Customer
In the Partner Center console, click **Customer Management** > **Invite Customer** to enter the invitation page where you can send an invitation link to a potential customer to bind with you.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/717bd37158414d0d2dba9acfe9ef5874.png)
You can send the invitation link directly via email or copy it and then send it in other ways.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/8fd716ff24f686825de0953ca3ed7ed5.png)

### 2. Reviewing Customer
You need to review the binding application submitted by the potential customer and confirm whether it can be bound as your customer.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/82000cf558e7565df94965e7a1c19649.png)

### 3. Checking Rejection Record
If you reject the binding applications submitted by potential customers, you can check all the rejection records.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/a80d169981f5a9e769b6a09af461f275.png)
