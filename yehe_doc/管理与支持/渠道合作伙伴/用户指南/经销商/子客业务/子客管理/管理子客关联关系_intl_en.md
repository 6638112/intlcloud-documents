## Reseller's Customer Relationship Management

### 1. Inviting reseller's customer

You can invite a potential customer to become your customer, no matter whether it has registered for a Tencent Cloud account or not.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/077bbcbeab290ae5ee0517a65bb5386d.png)

### 2. Reviewing reseller's customer

You need to review the binding application submitted by the potential customer and confirm whether it can be bound as your customer.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/75704cad8640d93a2fce2bce9d3ca1d1.png)

### 3. Viewing rejection record

If you reject the binding applications submitted by potential customers, you can view all rejection records.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/86fa5bef8324de5a4409d10c1e5f04b9.png)
