### Customer bill management

#### 1. Customer bill query
A customer bill query page is added. You can log in to the console, select **Channel Management**, and click **Customer Bills Management** on the left sidebar to enter the customer bills management page.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/af5367e001e8f149b04bd378f59616ec.png)

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/d00e8c2085c02f9b0d45035eeffa30f8.png)

Select a bill month and a customer UIN/name to display the credit of the specified customer in the specified month.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/ad9850b96bdf7132002017a57ceab370.png)

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/1665320830e6c86c02081152723ae6d3.png)

#### 2. Customer bill download
You can click the download icon in the top right of the bill list page to download a customer's bills in specified months.

>!
- As the bill of the current month changes in real time, it cannot be downloaded.
- When a historical monthly bill is downloaded, the full data of all fields in the month will be downloaded (that is, filtering and custom field settings will be invalidated during the download).

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/cbdd3f6c43475425571301137e1f6057.png)

#### 3. Customer bill confirmation
You can confirm historical bills of a customer. To do so, click **Bill Confirmation** in the top-right corner of the customer bills management page to enter the **Bill Confirmation** page.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/aa37718bd92597e15b244d80343d5ea6.png)

Query the monthly bills of the customer by month and customer UIN.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/af8b66bcc894dd7acd929706c1ce6009.png)

You can click **Confirm Bill** on the right of the list to confirm that the customer has made the payment for the month. After the confirmation, the status of the bill will become **Confirmed**, and the confirmed amount will be displayed.
