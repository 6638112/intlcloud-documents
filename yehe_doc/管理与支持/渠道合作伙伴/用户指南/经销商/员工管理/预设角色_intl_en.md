## Preset Role
Tencent Cloud provides preset roles and permissions for you to select when you associate your employee with a role.
The preset roles and permissions will be automatically initialized when you enter Partner Center. Before the initialization is completed, the **Employee Management** module is temporarily unaccessible.

### Preset role list
| Preset Role | Preset Permission | 
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| System admin | Global permission in Partner Center | 
| Customer manager | Permission to invite and manage second-level resellers and customers; permission to view customer consumption details, commission details, vouchers, and bills | 
| Finance management | Permission to maintain company information and manage second-level credits, voucher assets, and statements | 
| Partner Center development | Permission to invite customers to Partner Center and call credit management APIs |
