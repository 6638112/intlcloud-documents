## Commission Details

### Overview
The commission details comply with Tencentâ€™s commission incentive policies. A reseller can check the details data, which is consistent with the statement data.

### Directions
1. Log in to the Tencent Cloud console with a reseller account.
2. Go to **Partner Center -> Commission Management -> Commission Details**
3. Perform operations on commission details as needed.
  - View details: Click to view the commission details by month. The commission amount showed in commission details is the same as the total amount of multiple commission statements.
  - Download: Click to download the commission details data for the specified month.
