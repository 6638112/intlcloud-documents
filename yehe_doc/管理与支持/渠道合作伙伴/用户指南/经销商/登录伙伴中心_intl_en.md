### Partner Center Login

Step 1. Log in to your [reseller account](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/login).

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/8bf71e6530c1fcacdef9147748f69035.png)

Step 2. Click **Console** in the top-right corner.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/6e9f607ee502d89eb33d3f5eeba104bc.png)

Step 3. Hover over the account name and click **Channel Management** to enter the channel console.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/0970e2cf8cb23c0bc1087ed7daf5878b.png)
