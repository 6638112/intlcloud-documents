### Partner Center Login

Step 1. Log in to your [reseller account](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/).

![img](https://docimg3.docs.qq.com/image/aYs7uKuoKECk0Fy32a8Mgw?w=1012&h=362)

Step 2. Click **Console** in the top-right corner.

![img](https://docimg10.docs.qq.com/image/9NQ7jxjTe3E_DJHoSgltJA?w=1058&h=280)

Step 3. Hover over the account name and click **Channel Management** to enter the channel console.

![img](https://docimg10.docs.qq.com/image/KLK8gFG_PN8RbLrdxUKCuQ?w=1120&h=708)
