### Discount Management

Tencent Cloud resellers are eligible for the partner discount.

 ##### 1. Apply for partner discounts

Negotiate your discounts offline with your Tencent Cloud sales rep, who will provide you with a complete quotation, including discounts, offers, and other information.

##### 2. View partner discounts

You currently can't view discounts online. Contact your sales rep for help.

##### 3. Adjust partner discounts

Contact your sales rep for negotiation and adjustment.
