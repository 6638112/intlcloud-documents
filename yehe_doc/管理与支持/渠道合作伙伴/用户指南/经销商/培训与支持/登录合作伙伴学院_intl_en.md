### Logging in to Partner Academy
Step 1. Go to [Tencent Cloud website](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com) .

Step 2. Click **Partners** on the topbar to redirect to the Partner Academy as shown below:

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/9f826754da1c9ddf87b81fbce81fdefe.png)


>? 
- You need to log in to your Tencent Cloud account before entering the Partner Academy.
- You can log in to the Partner Academy only with a Tencent Cloud International partner root account but not a partner sub-account, collaborator account, or other accounts.
- If you don't meet the requirement for partner root account, you can ask your partner to apply for access on your behalf. For more information, see [Application for Partner Academy Access](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1085/43743).

Step 3. After entering the Partner Academy, you will be automatically redirected to the Partner Academy homepage.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/d8788d30328bf6a1f0cef756f86e2820.png)
