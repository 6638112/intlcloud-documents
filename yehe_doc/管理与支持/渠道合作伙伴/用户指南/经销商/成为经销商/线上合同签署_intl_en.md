>- The online signing process starts after the reseller registration is approved.
>- New resellers must sign the contract before they can log in to the Partner console.

### Contract signing process
1. Go to the contract management page (the contract to be signed will be automatically pushed to new partners) and click **Sign** to enter the signing process.
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/c264b9c9e9212be15db9d3f6e20cdd9a.png)

2. On the information and verification page, verify whether the partner information entered during registration is accurate (if not, contact your channel manager for information change). Then, select the target countries/regions. After verifying the information, click **Next** to enter the contract signing page.
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/24a83ccc215ffcadd92ba1eabe746788.png)
3. View the contract details, indicate that you have read the contract, and click **Sign**.
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/04a55b5134730f4b0cd4a37d1be54385.png)
   
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/fd57bccd05a7acb944f9c17b137efb6a.png)
4. After successful submission, you can return to the list page and click **Refresh** to view the contract signing status (it takes 1–2 minutes for the system to sign the contract).
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/61b54331b0bc69e62f8d11f34d32d0a8.png)

5. You can click **View details** to view the signed contract.
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/26c91516270f146696091b82f91172e4.png)

### Contract non-renewal
1. After the contract is signed successfully, you can perform a non-renewal operation and verify the current contract information. (Note: You cannot perform this operation within 30 days before the contract expiration date.)
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/a481a948861dad7f647bd70da6b52818.png)
2. After successful submission, the contract status will change to **In review**, and you can refresh the current page to view the latest status.(For approval details, please contact your channel manager.)
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/61b54331b0bc69e62f8d11f34d32d0a8.png)
3. After your application for non-renewal is approved, the system will not push the renewal contract upon the expiration of the current contract.
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/197d8605cb7427578f5f14c4417b6865.png)

### Contract renewal
1. You can renew the contract by clicking **Renew** 45 days before the contract expires.（You have 15 days to renew the contract. If you don’t do so 30 days before the expiration of the contract, the contract will be automatically renewed by default.）
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/01bc673f243284097b0217bbc2a38dd1.png)
2. After confirming the renewal, you will be redirected to the renewal contract signing process. (The renewal contract will take effect as of the date following the expiration of the current contract.)
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/e6212441b7b06159785eb0e829d4b22a.png)
   
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/3bb65135533fb813c10f70885d04d982.png)
