As a distributor, you can query the list of all your resellers and view the basic information and available credits of them.
Step 1. Log in to [Tencent Cloud](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/login) with your distributor account and enter the [Partner Center](https://console.intl.cloud.tencent.com/partners).
Step 2. On the left sidebar, select **Reseller Business**.
Step 3. Manage your resellers.

### Querying Resellers
You can query your reseller by its account ID or name.
