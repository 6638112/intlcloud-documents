### Querying Reseller Bills
Step 1. Log in to [Tencent Cloud](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/login) with your distributor account and enter the [Partner Center](https://console.intl.cloud.tencent.com/partners).
Step 2. On the left sidebar, select **Reseller Business** > **Reseller Bills** to enter the reseller bills page.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/e5fe7734301a428dbddf35c00bace18e.png)
Step 3. Select a billing month and a reseller account ID/name to check its bill for that month.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/452cb57b4e305f2db1b8bb36b9149c22.png)
