## Customer bill

### 1. View bills

Step 1:Customer can log in to the [Console > Billing Center](https://console.intl.cloud.tencent.com/expense/customerbill)  to view your credit

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/57afda34ff0aeb330d6e92369f85360d.png)

Step 2:Bills can be filtered by month with the calendar at the top of the page.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/762a717f1891be0713dfb9a4af1adb03.png)

Step 3:In the filter area, you can filter bills by product, project, region, etc.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/76f9afa7c58e2baef9238c85386086fa.png)

Step 4:You can click the settings icon above the list to configure the fields displayed on the list page.

### 2. Bill description
   (1) The billing time is about one hour. For a monthly subscribed product, its bill will be generated about one hour after the purchase. For a pay-as-you-go product, its bill will be generated about one hour after the product settlement cycle.

   (2) The bill of the current month may be delayed for real-time push network reasons. The displayed monthly bill shall prevail, which can be viewed on the third day of the next month.

   (3) Voucher deduction information is not displayed in the customer bills.

   (4) For products purchased offline, the product team will generate reseller bills and forward them to channel managers.
