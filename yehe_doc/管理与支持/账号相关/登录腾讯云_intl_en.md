## Introduction
This document describes how to log in to Tencent Cloud after signing up for an account.

>All the Tencent Cloud accounts mentioned herein are root accounts. If you want to log in to Tencent Cloud with a sub-account, please do so as instructed in CAM > [Logging in as a Sub-User](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/598/34006).

An email address can be used to log in to Tencent Cloud.
The section below describes how to use an email address to log in to Tencent Cloud.

## Directions

### Logging in with an email address
1. Go to [Tencent Cloud](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/) and click **Log In** in the top-right corner of the homepage.
2. Click **Email** in "Other Login Means" under the QR code.
3. Enter the email address and password set when you signed up for your Tencent Cloud account and click **Sign In** to log in to Tencent Cloud.
