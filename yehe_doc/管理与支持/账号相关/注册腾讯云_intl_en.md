1. On the [sign-up page](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/account/register), select the type of Tencent Cloud account (business or personal) you want to create, enter the information required, and click **Next**.

>? Each mobile number can be used for at most five accounts. When the limit is reached, you can modify the mobile number bound to an existing account and try again. For detailed directions, see [Modifying Mobile Number and Email Address](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/36000).

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/6f0e489dbcfff16af04e59e29f6e6b90.png)

2. Enter your account information, read the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy and, if you agree, select the boxes and click **Next**.

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/228ee9e45e75d270a56efc48d290b443.png)

3. Enter your credit card information and click **Bind Credit Card**. 

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/0ee33d34a4fbb1f8c42e092b6f473e37.png)

4. After the credit card is bound successfully, click **Continue** to complete your sign-up.

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/7f6d1ea96add8800c5c36b76565a852d.png)

5. Log in to the [console](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/login) with your account and start exploring Tencent Cloud products.

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/fd807364118f6e49d48541985ed7576e.png)

After sign-up, please add a verification mobile number in the console as soon as possible. Without a verification mobile number, you may be unable to use some Tencent Cloud services.

Verification mobile number: The mobile number used for identity verification, which is an account protection method. For details, see [Verifying and Modifying Your Verification Mobile Number](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/48918).

Contact mobile number: The mobile number that receives messages from Tencent Cloud, including notifications about overdue payments, resource expiration, and events. For details, see [Verifying and Modifying Your Contact Mobile Number](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/48919).
