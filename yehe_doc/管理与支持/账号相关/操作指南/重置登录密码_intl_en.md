## Directions
### Step 1. Go to the “Forgot password” page
1. Go to the [Tencent Cloud website](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/) and click **Log In** in the top right corner.
2. On the login page, click **Forgot password**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/c341af8c6a328e10a14a96a56b2fc788.png)

### Step 2. Enter your email address
Enter your account email and click **Next**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/32f86a31f59a6d314b0b5e154146287b.png)

### Step 3. Check your inbox
We will send a password reset email to your inbox. Click **Reset Password** or copy the link in the email and paste it in a browser.

### Step 4. Set a new password
On the password resetting page, enter a new password and confirm it. Click **Submit**.

### Step 5. Verify your identity
In the pop-up window, verify your identity as required to complete the resetting process.
