## Verifying Your Contact Mobile Number

1. Log in to the Tencent Cloud console and go to [Account Information](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/developer). Find **Contact Number** and click **Verify Now** on the right.

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/1d792d1b40b3d284b42896dd3e04d150.png)

2. You will be directed to the **User List** page. Click **Replacing** next to **Contact Number**. If you want to continue using the current number, click **Resend Verification Link**.

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/a8ac5f12282c047a8422c9c7e454aefc.png)

3. If you want to use a different number, click **Cancel Change** and then click the edit icon on the right.

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/091cd9f62db7e107f53deed4e44ba4db.png)

4. Enter a valid mobile number and click **OK**.

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/7805fc72e2ba22077ddd0c35878a23c9.png)

5. An SMS message will be sent to the number you entered. Click the link in the message to complete the verification.

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/9820e30b41a35f474a0976f678f7729b.png)



## Modifying Your Contact Mobile Number

1. Log in to the Tencent Cloud console and go to [Account Information](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/developer). Find **Contact Number** and click the edit icon on the right.

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/cadfc07c4da07bb27f65ee2592f6b8c9.png)

2. You will be directed to the **User List** page. Click the edit icon next to **Contact Number** and enter the new mobile number you want to use in the pop-up window.

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/dc4172572900af9311c455ca73f593fd.png)

3. An SMS message will be sent to the number you entered. Click the link in the message to verify the number.

   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/0c926f0b61a24f49525c8850ddc030e5.png)
