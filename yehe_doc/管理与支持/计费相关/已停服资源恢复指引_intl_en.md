## Pay-As-You-Go Products

You can top up your account via **Billing Center** > [**Payment**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/account/recharge). When your account balance is positive, pay-as-you-go resources will be restored automatically.
For detailed directions on top-up, see [Top-up Process](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/555/11319).

If your pay-as-you-go resources are not restored automatically after you top up, please [submit a ticket](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/workorder/category).
