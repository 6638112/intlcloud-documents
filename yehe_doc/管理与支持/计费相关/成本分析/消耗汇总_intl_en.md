You can view the consumption summary and details by product, project, region, or resource ID on the [Consumption Summary](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/expense/cost/summary) page.


### Summary by Product
The monthly cost details are summarized by product. You can view the monthly cost of each product and the cost change from the previous month.

You can click a product to enter the summary by resource page and view the resource details of the product.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/a2c6f09da2ff8aafa5d9bc5b0e70e649.png)

### Summary by Project
The monthly cost details are summarized by project. You can view the monthly cost of each project and the cost change from the previous month.

You can click a project to view all products under it as well as their respective costs and change from the previous month.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/59001b5a80286a930d1859edd8ea0fe0.png)

### Summary by Region
The monthly cost details are summarized by region. You can view the monthly cost in each region and change from the previous month.

You can click a region to view all products under it as well as their respective costs and change from the previous month.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/ae3e3febc93cc4d29e929216ddce6299.png)

### Summary by Resource ID
The monthly cost details are summarized by resource ID. You can view the monthly cost, project, region, billing mode, and bill details of each resource.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/d1823283091864edd976461d7ec23354.png)

Select the target resource ID and click **Bill Details** to enter the bill details page of the resource and view its monthly cost details, billable items, usage, cost ratio, and cost trend in the past 6 months.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/ad1367990986df2051427dc0946b7f96.png)
