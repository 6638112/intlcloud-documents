[Consumption Overview](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/expense/cost/overview) displays monthly consumption trends, total cost, cost by product, top 10 projects by cost, cost by region, etc.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/3311091140f8f58bb85b3c9683188cbd.png)

## Cost Trend

You can view your consumption trends in the last 6 and 12 months. The cost type can be total cost, cash payment, complimentary cash, or vouchers.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/5b22ebbd986775c612bc67fd37771566.png)

## Total Cost
Total cost is your total monthly consumption. The calculation formula is **Total cost = Cash payment + Complimentary cash + Vouchers**. The priority of account payments is vouchers, complimentary cash, and cash.

Click **View Details** to enter the consumption summary page and view the cost details.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/f025b5a07f3c574ace21f9fdbea6a4dd.png)

## Cost by Product
This section displays your monthly cost details by product. You can view each product's monthly cost and its percentage in the total.

Click **All Products** to enter the consumption summary page and view the cost details by product.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/33d0f8283072e8fb432874dfc5c2eb0a.png)

## Top 10 Projects
This section displays your monthly cost details by project. You can view the 10 projects you spend the most on.

Click **All Projects** to enter the consumption summary page and view the cost details by project.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/e43d060a3dfd39e60f593552ca5ac2fd.png)

## Cost by Region
This section displays your monthly cost details by region. You can view each region's monthly cost and its percentage in the total.

Click **All Regions** to enter the consumption summary page and view the cost details by region.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/9bf7ff66db59e73ac6ae463459d345a4.png)
