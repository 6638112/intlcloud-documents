## Submit Partner Application

1.Go to the https://intl.cloud.tencent.com, and select [Partners] > [Become a partner].

2.Go to the application page, and you will be redirected to a new page to fill in your company information. 

1)Fill in the company information

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/12a4b5016f9c3f91d9c05efd1f60b0f4.png)

2)Fill in the company bank information

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/2a58a66e001a4a1de0031eff3be2305a.png)

3)Fill in the contact information

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/2edf8cf837894423960e5aabc14691f4.png)

3.After completing the information, click [Submit].

**Notes:**

- Please make sure the company name is same with that on the business license.
- Please make sure the provided bank information is same with that on the bank materials.
- Please make sure the company name in verification is same with that submitted in the partner application.
