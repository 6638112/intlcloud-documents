## Scenario
This document describes how to delete clusters that are no longer needed from the TKE console to avoid unnecessary costs. On the **Delete Cluster** page, you can view all the resources of a cluster, terminated resources, and can choose whether to retain some resources as needed. Ensure that you are fully aware of the risks of deleting clusters.



## Directions
1. Log in to the [TKE console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/tke2) and click **Cluster** in the left sidebar.
2. On the **Cluster Management** page, choose **More** > **Delete** on the right of the target cluster, as shown in the following figure.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/01debd945b31b5f5feb903e5b7656ce3.png)
3. In the **Delete Cluster** window that appears, choose to retain or delete existing resources of the cluster as needed, as shown in the following figure.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/948899a6185cff4aa9905aa967abe798.png)
4. Read the risks of cluster deletion and select "I have read the above information and hereby confirm deletion of the cluster".
5. Click **OK**.
