
This document describes how to manage private and public templates.


## Directions


Log in to the TIC console and go to the [**Templates**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/tic/templates) page. On this page, you can manage private and public templates.

#### Private templates

You can create, use, copy, and delete private templates.
1. Click the **Private** tab.
2. To create a stack from a private template, select a private template and click **Deploy**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/0e8af12c2ed727f7cdab99313bd7ec43.png)


#### Public templates

You can query, save, and deploy public templates.
1. Click the **Public** tab.
2. To create a stack from a public template, select a public template and click **Deploy**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/4cdc19dcd0e0742c909ee9972c1af17f.png)
