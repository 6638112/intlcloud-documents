#### Daily OPS

Advisor can regularly assess the health of cloud resources, rate the risks of cloud architectures and resources, and offer optimization suggestions online to help you improve business continuity.

#### Sales Campaigns

Advisor can assess the service usage in advance to check the stress level of cloud resources. In this way, you can submit a ticket as needed to add more resources based on the estimated campaign scale and ensure the smooth operation of campaigns.


#### Proactive Architecture Optimization

Advisor can verify the security, fault tolerance, and backup capabilities of architectures to ensure the continuous and stable operation of businesses. You can actively trigger inspections as needed.
