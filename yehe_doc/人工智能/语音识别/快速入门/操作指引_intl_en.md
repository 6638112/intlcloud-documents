### Logging In to Console
Log in to the Tencent Cloud console. If you don't have an account yet, please sign up first as instructed in [Signing up for a Tencent Cloud Account](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/17985) and complete identity verification.

### Activating Service
Log in to the [ASR console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/asr) and activate the service.

### Free Tier and Purchase
Upon ASR service activation, you will be given a free tier of API calls, which will be added to your Tencent Cloud account as a resource package and deducted first during billing. Any usage exceeding the free tier will be pay-as-you-go. For more information, see [Billing Overview](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1118/43352).
