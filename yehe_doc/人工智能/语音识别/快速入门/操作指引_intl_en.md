### Logging in to the Console
Log in to the Tencent Cloud console. If you don't have an account yet, sign up first as instructed in [Signing Up](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/17985).

### Activating Service
Log in to the [ASR console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/asr) and activate the service.

### Making a Purchase
ASR services are pay-as-you-go once activated. For more information, see [Billing Overview](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1118/43352).
