To use Tencent Cloud Weiling, please log in at the Tencent Cloud official website and submit your [application form](https://cloud.tencent.com/apply/p/06f15o860q2u). The application process is as shown below:

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/c06cd908f0c2f9843ffd4b97387704ad.svg)
### Step 1. Log in
Log in at the Tencent Cloud official website. If you don't have an account yet, please sign up as instructed in [Signing up for a Tencent Cloud Account](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/17985).

### Step 2. Submit application
Go to the [Tencent Cloud Weiling service overview page](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/products/weiling), click **Apply Now**, fill out the application form, and click **Submit Application**.

### Step 3. Wait for approval
After receiving your application, we will review it and contact you to confirm your preliminary needs and arrange business negotiation.
>?It takes about 3–5 days to review your application. Please wait patiently.

### Step 4. Confirm needs
After the preliminary communication about your needs, service version confirmation, and deployment environment assessment, we will make detailed deployment plans for your network environment and IoT hardware.

### Step 5. Purchase service
After we provide the building deployment plans to you, you can sign the service contract with us.

### Step 6. Deploy service
After you purchase the service, we will deploy relevant hardware devices such as cameras in your building, connect the software and hardware to the cloud, and complete other relevant configurations.

### Step 7. Perform service acceptance
After we complete the relevant configuration and deployment, you should inspect and accept the [Tencent Cloud Weiling](https://m.welink.qq.com/#/index) service.
