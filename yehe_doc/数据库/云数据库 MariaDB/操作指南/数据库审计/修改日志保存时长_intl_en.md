
This document describes how to modify the log retention period after the database audit service is activated.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [TencentDB for MariaDB console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/mariadb), select **Database Audit** on the left sidebar, select a region at the top, and click the **Audit Log** tab.
2. In the top-right corner of the **Audit Log** tab, click **Configure**.
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/5f93d2a4440298529b363a1291f98668.png)
3. In the pop-up window, modify the log retention period and click **Submit**.
