
## Overview
You can delete a stopped or failed task. Once deleted, the task will no longer exist or occupy any resources. 

## Prerequisites
The migration task has been stopped or failed. 

## Directions
1. Log in to the [DTS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/dts/migration), select **Data Migration** on the left sidebar, select the target migration task, and click **More** > **Delete** in the **Operation** column.
2. In the pop-up window, confirm that everything is correct and click **OK**.
