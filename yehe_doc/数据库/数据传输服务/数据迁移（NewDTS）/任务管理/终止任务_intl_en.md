
## Overview
You can terminate a started migration task.
>!Note that once terminated, the task cannot be resumed, and executed operations cannot be rolled back.

## Directions
Log in to the [DTS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/dts/migration), select **Data Migration** on the left sidebar, select the target migration task, and click **More** > **Terminate** in the **Operation** column.
