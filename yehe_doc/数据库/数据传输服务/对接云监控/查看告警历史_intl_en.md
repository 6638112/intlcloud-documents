
## Overview
You can view the historical metric and event alarms to understand the performance metrics of the system.  

## Viewing Metric Alarm
1. Log in to the [CM console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/monitor).
2. Click **Alarm Configuration** > **Alarm Policy**, select the target alarm policy, and click **Alarm Records** in the **Operation** column.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/a0030883d87b442c51af11e542d68a95.png)

## Viewing Event Alarm
1. Log in to the [CM console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/monitor).
2. Click **Event Center** > **Product Event** to view the target alarming event.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/fa9248e47cbcce34143451d067c84837.png)
