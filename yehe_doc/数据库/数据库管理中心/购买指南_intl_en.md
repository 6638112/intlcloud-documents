Database Management Center (DMC) is now in open beta testing, and you can try all its features free of charge.

You can access DMC using the following two methods:

### Method 1
Directly log in to the [DMC console](https://bj-dmc.cloud.tencent.com/v2/qcloudLogin/login).
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/e4c0710c3eea7379f8cc170e9a90a6ef.png)

### Method 2
Log in to the [TencentDB Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cdb) and click **Log In** in the "Operation" column of the instance list to access DMC.
