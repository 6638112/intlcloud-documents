The compliance audit overview page displays the risk trend of a single audit group at different times, as well as the rankings of operations, total risks, and high, medium, and low risk counts of different audit groups.

Log in to the [DBbrain console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/dbbrain/security-audit), click **Database Security** > **Compliance Audit** on the left sidebar, and select **Overview** at the top to view the **Real-Time** or **Historical** audit data.
