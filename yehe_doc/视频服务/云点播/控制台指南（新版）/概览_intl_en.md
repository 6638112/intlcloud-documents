Log in to the LVB Console and click **[Overview](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/live/livestat)** on the left sidebar to enter the LVB overview page.
On the LVB overview page, you can view yesterday's traffic fees, real-time data of live streaming, number of online viewers, and the trends in billable bandwidth and traffic in the last few days, switch the billing mode, and change the time granularity.

#### 1. Switch the billing mode
On the **Yesterday's Traffic Usage** tab, click **Switch** to pop up the billing mode switch reminder, and click **OK**
>To learn more about prices, click **[Pricing Overview](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/267/2819)** in the prompt.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/e453bf47bbd6bfb9d0202dcdc9539c86.png)


#### 2. Change the time granularity
You can change the time granularity used when viewing the trends in billable bandwidth or traffic by selecting the desired option in the drop-down list to the right of **Interval**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/924b21e740f525317441b0feb14f95dc.png)
