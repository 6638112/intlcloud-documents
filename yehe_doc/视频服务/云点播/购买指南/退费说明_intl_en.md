## 5 days no reason to refund

In order to make it easier for you to use the Video-on-Demand, if the resources in the Video-on-Demand resource package you purchased have not been used, we support a refund within 5 days without reason.

## Refund policy[](id:p1)

- For a single account, the unused Video-on-Demand resource package can be refunded within 5 days (including 5 days) from the date of new purchase for 5 days without reason, and will not be refunded once used.
- Once the short video license is activated, it cannot be refunded (after the resource pack and the short video license are bound, the resource pack cannot be refunded and is considered used).
- In case of suspected abnormality or malicious refund, Tencent Cloud has the right to reject your refund application.
- For orders that meet the 5-day no-reason refund scenario, the refund amount is the entire consumption amount spent at the time of purchase, including the amount in the cash account, the amount transferred to the account, and the amount in the gift account.

>!
>- A single account only supports one refund without reason.
>- Credits or vouchers are not refundable.
>- The refund amount will be refunded to your Tencent Cloud account in the original way.

## Refund Steps

1. Log in - Tencent Cloud, click the corresponding number of available resource packs under the resource pack module on the service overview page.
2. Select the resource pack that needs to be refunded on the resource pack page, and click Return to the right of the selected resource pack.

>!  Before returning the resource pack, please check whether the resource pack complies with the refund rules. If so, click Return. If not, it cannot be returned.
