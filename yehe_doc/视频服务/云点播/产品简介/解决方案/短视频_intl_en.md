## UGSV Application
### Foreword
Mobile UGSV businesses have a wide variety of use cases in ecommerce shopping, entertainment video, and video software. Tencent Cloud VOD provides a one-stop UGSV solution covering all stages from video generation, upload, and processing to distribution and playback, which enables you to quickly implement and launch your mobile UGSV application while focusing on your business development.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/b16717881c4568c6bcc3885518267609.png)

In the UGSV use case, Tencent Cloud VOD's UGSV SDK provides a rich set of features such as capturing and shoot, editing and splicing, special effect production, reverb and subtitling, and dynamic stickers. With the support of VOD's powerful backend capabilities, you can easily build UGSV applications. In addition, the UGSV SDK also provides:
- Various filters, beauty filter effects, and dynamic stickers to reduce the difficulty of development and improve the user experience.
- UGSV demos, open-source code, and comments to make it easier for you to better understand and use the UGSV SDK.

### Directions
1. Sign up: sign up for a Tencent Cloud account and activate the VOD service as instructed in [Getting Started - Step 1. Activate VOD](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/266/8757).
2. Activate the license: [add the UGSV license](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1069/38041) in the VOD console and enter the information.
3. Download: [download the application source code](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1069/37914) and debug, compile, and run it as instructed in [Quickly Building UGSV Application](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1069/38004).
