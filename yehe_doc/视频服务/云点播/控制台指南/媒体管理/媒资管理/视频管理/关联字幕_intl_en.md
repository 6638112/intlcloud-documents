This document describes how to bind subtitles to or unbind subtitles from an adaptive bitrate streaming template in the [VOD console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vod).

>?
>- Subtitle binding is currently in beta testing and is free of charge.
>- You need to bind an adaptive bitrate streaming template to a video before you can bind subtitles to the video.
>- You may be charged for using other features (such as storage, transcoding, and video playback acceleration).

## Directions
### Step 1. Bind an adaptive bitrate streaming template
1. Log in to the [VOD console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vod), select the target application, and select **Media Assets > Video Management** on the left sidebar.
2. Select the video you want to bind subtitles to and click **Process Video** above the list. A "Process Video" window appears.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/b63ce885ce233a3de842956195b7477e.png)
3. Select **Adaptive Bitrate Streaming** for **Processing Type**, select a template, and click **Confirm**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/7b577c165763b122b6dfae7e1bddbc71.png)

### Step 2. Bind subtitles
1. Click the name of the target video to go to the **Video Management** page. In **Adaptive Bitrate Streaming List** under the **Basic Info** tab, click **Add Subtitle Set**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/5ee7966f2921877d670d89a43bad8e42.png)
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/0adb411ebc1e7b83248d4adefc1bd9e4.png)
2. Select the subtitles you added and click **Bind Subtitle Set**. In the window that pops up, select an adaptive bitrate streaming template, and click **Confirm**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/f428fb18e45aa76cb2ca15d423b1c834.png)
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/64ee3656a60a7e07f4aed24613659c84.png)

>? Click **Delete** to unbind subtitles from an adaptive bitrate streaming template.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/c56a2f3d6ef53eef648f33c808157427.png)
