## Overview
This document describes how to delete audio/video files in the VOD console.
>! Deleted videos can no longer be accessed from CDN nodes. **The deletion cannot be undone**.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [VOD console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vod) and select **Application Management** on the left sidebar.

2. Select the target application.

3. Go to **Media Assets > Video/Audio Management > Uploaded**.

4. Find the target audio/video file and click **Delete** in the **Operation** column. In the pop-up window, click **Confirm**. You can also select multiple files, click **Batch Delete**, and then click **Confirm** in the pop-up window.
