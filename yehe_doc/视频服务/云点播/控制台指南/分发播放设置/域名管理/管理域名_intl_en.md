You can perform certificate management on existing domain names.

## Certificate Management
1. Log in to the [VOD console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vod) and click **Distribution and Playback** > **Domain Name** on the left sidebar.
2. Click **Set** in the row of the target domain name to enter the configuration page.
3. Click **Access Control**, and then toggle **Use Certificate** on or off.	
>! A certificate can be set only for a custom domain name.
