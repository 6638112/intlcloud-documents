## Overview
This document describes how to delete videos in the VOD console.
> !Once deleted, a video will be completely removed from Tencent Cloud in a **non-recoverable** manner and be inaccessible from CDN nodes.

## Directions
1. Log in to the VOD console and select **Media Assets** > **[Video Management](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vod/media)**. The "Uploaded" page is displayed.
2. Click **Delete** in the row of the target video file. In the pop-up window, click **Confirm** to delete the video. Alternatively, select the target video files, click **Batch Delete** above the list, and click **Confirm** in the pop-up window to delete all of them in batches.
