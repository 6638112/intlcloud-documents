## Overview
This document describes how to modify video categories in the VOD Console.


## Directions

1. Log in to the VOD Console and select **Media Assets** > **[Video Management](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vod/media)** to enter the "Uploaded" page by default.
2. Select the target video, click **Modify Video Category** above the list, select the category into which to put it, and click **Confirm**.
