## Overview
This document describes how to filter video files in the VOD console.

## Directions
1. Log in to the VOD console and select **Media Assets** > **[Video Management](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vod/media)** to enter the **Uploaded** page by default.
2. In the search box above the list, select a **media asset attribute** or enter a **keyword** to filter the uploaded files.
	![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/e300e242921d2949ef3e32ae607770d0.png)
	>?If you don't select any attribute, the filename will be matched by default.
