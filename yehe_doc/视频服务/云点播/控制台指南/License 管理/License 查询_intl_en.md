The license query page displays the basic information of the UGSV SDK licenses, including the trial and purchased licenses.




- **Type**: displays the types and number of licenses used under the account.
- **Claim/Purchase Time**: indicates the time when a license is obtained.
- **Expiration Time**: indicates the expiration time of a license.
- **Status**: divides into "Expired" and "Available".


>!A license will be deemed as used and cannot be refunded once activated or bound.
