## Value-Added Services

TRTC provides two additional features, **On-cloud recording** and **On-cloud MixTranscoding**, in addition to the four basic services. These two value-added services must be used in conjunction with the basic services and will incur additional fees. Their pricing is detailed below:

| Billable Service                                                         | Description                                                     | Billing Details                                                     |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| [On-cloud recording](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/647/35426) | TRTC leverages the capabilities of **CSS** to allow you to record entire live streaming sessions through relayed push and save the recording files in **VOD** | [On-cloud recording billing](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/647/38385) |
| [On-Cloud MixTranscoding](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/647/34618) | TRTC uses an MCU cluster to mix and transcode the audio and video streams in a room and pushes the transcoded stream to CSS to enable **on-cloud recording** or **CDN live streaming**. | [On-cloud MixTranscoding billing](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/647/38929) |
