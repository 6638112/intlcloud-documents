TRTC supports two payment modes: prepaid packages and pay-as-you-go. The former is the default mode. To switch to the pay-as-you-go mode, please see [Enabling Pay-As-You-Go]. You can use the [TRTC Price Calculator](https://buy.intl.cloud.tencent.com/pricing/trtc) to estimate your usage of TRTC services and the cost. This document describes how to purchase prepaid packages.

## Prerequisites

Before you purchase a TRTC package, you must [sign up](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/17985) for a Tencent Cloud account and [contact us](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/contact-us) to add your account to the allowlist.

## Directions

1. Go to the [TRTC prepaid package purchase page](https://buy.cloud.tencent.com/trtc) and log in.
2. Select a **Package Type** and **Package Duration**.
	- Fixed-time package:
	<img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/02015d595359b2cf445c1f4a3ecce274.png" width="700px"/>
	- Custom package:
	<img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/9fe8306d025ac6cc419c8cd109f055c4.png" width="700px"/>
3. Check **I have read and agree to the TRTC [Service Level Agreement](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/647/35661)**, and click **Purchase**.
4. Confirm your information and complete the payment.
