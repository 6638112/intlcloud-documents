## Rich Feature Modules
In addition to basic features such as beauty filters, makeups, and stickers, it is also capable of accurate portrait keying and segmentation, which enable users to customize and blur the background in images and videos.

## Awesome Beauty Filters
It offers seven face shaping filters (three for face slimming and one for face shape, chin slimming, face narrowing, and face shortening each), bringing a superb face beautification experience where the entire face can be adjusted in multiple dimensions with great freedom.

## Stunning Filters

It displays gorgeous filters with industry-leading 3D capabilities, where animated stickers can always stay on facial keypoints to easily deliver an immersive and interactive sticker experience.

## Powerful Material Creation Tools
It offers an excellent massive material library and rich AI features, such as 3D capabilities, process control, and animated effect import, making effect customization effortless.

## Ease to Use
It can be efficiently integrated into MLVB, TRTC, and UGSV to apply effects in a simplified and rapid way. For detailed directions, see [Demo](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1143/45374).
