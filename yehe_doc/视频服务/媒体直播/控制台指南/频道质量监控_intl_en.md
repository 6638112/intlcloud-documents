
Click a channel in “Channel Management” to go to its details page, where you can view all the input, output, alerts, health and other information of the channel.
MediaLive will generate an alert for a channel when a problem or a potential problem occurs in any pipeline of the channel. 

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/35f6b92e513e1063e53de64c686c2796.jpg)

In Health, you can view the channel input and output information and the corresponding curves to determine whether the current channel is working properly. 

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/084b11ecdeb275553b4e0b6faf566439.jpg)
