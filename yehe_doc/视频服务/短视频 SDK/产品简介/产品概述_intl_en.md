
In recent years, the user generated short video (UGSV) industry is booming and has become the most promising field after live streaming with its creative features and fresh video contents. To help you quickly create a UGSV application, Tencent Cloud VOD provides a one-stop UGSV solution covering all stages from video generation, upload, and processing to distribution and playback, enabling you to quickly launch your UGSV application.

Based on Tencent Cloud's powerful VOD capabilities in upload, storage, transcoding, and distribution, the UGSV solution not only provides client SDKs that feature capturing, editing, splicing, special effect creating, sharing, and playback of audio and video clips, but also integrates Tencent's IM, social networking, and user profile data as well as AI-based face recognition and image detection technologies, allowing you to focus on your core business and implement your mobile UGSV application with speed and ease.

This document describes how to try out, activate, purchase, and connect to the Tencent Cloud UGSV service in the fastest and simplest way.
