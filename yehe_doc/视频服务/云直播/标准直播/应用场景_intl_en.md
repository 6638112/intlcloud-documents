## Online Education

LVB provides a one-stop service for online education. Your well-crafted teaching materials can be uploaded and downloaded through the agile console and APIs, and are protected with player passwords and dynamic URL-based hotlink protection. In addition, the open player APIs make it easy to implement various teaching features such as setting playback time limits and disallowing progress bar dragging.
