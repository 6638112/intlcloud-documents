## Notes
- Tencent Cloud key customers can contact sales to change billing modes.
- Users on the daily billing cycle can also contact sales to switch to the monthly billing cycle.
- Users can switch between the daily bill-by-traffic and daily bill-by-bandwidth modes via the LVB console. The new billing mode will take effect the next day after you finish configuring the billing mode switch. When switching from the daily billing cycle to the monthly billing cycle, the switch will take effect the next day after it receives approval. When switching from the monthly billing cycle to the daily billing cycle, the switch will take effect the next month after it receives approval.
- Users can switch between daily bill-by-traffic and daily bill-by-bandwidth only once a day.


## How to Change Your Billing Mode

1. Log in to the [LVB console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/live) and click **Overview** in the left sidebar.
2. Find **Billing Details** > **Yesterday's Traffic Usage**, and click **Switch**.
   ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/ee8e815c10fdf6dd8c9a95c04082471e.png)
3. Click **Confirm** to switch **Daily bill-by-traffic** to **Daily bill-by-bandwidth**.
   ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/f45e16f7fc10b4102cc420a8fd945253.png)
