Log in to the CSS console and go to **[Stream Management](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/live/streammanage)** to view **Live Streams**, **Stream History**, and **Disabled Streams**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/e8a433c19b3a86882eac03629526fb2c.png)

>! You can view stream history in the past 7 days under the **Stream History** tab or query records in the past month in **[Stream Interruption Records](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/live/tools/streamevent)**.

### Disabling a stream
Click **Disable** to disable a stream. Once a stream is disabled, you can no longer push it.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/9f056d15c34a32da0dd8c6e4b07de3fc.png)

### Enabling a stream
Find the target stream in **Disabled Streams**, and click **Enable** to enable it.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/d26a632f52f73b25efa27055c0c8a677.png)
