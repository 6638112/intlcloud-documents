### Monthly usage details
Under the **Live Screencapture** tab, you can view your **Monthly Usage** of live screencapture and **download** the statistics of a specific month in the last 6 months by clicking **Export Details**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/d2d7aef04f809a9a29bb0ea9cd97f53b.png)

### Usage in last 30 days

Under the **Live Transcoding** tab, you can view your usage of live transcoding in the last 30 days, including start time, end time, duration, codec, bitrate, and transcoding type.
