The licenses for our DRM encryption feature are provided by the third party licensing service SDMC. To use the feature, you must provide the UID, SecretID, and SecretKey you obtain from SDMC. This document shows you how to obtain the information.
 

## Directions

1. Register for [SDMC’s DRM service](https://www.xmediacloud.com/contact-us/).
2. SDMC will review your application and email you the address of its DRM console and your initial password.
3. Log in to [SDMC’s DRM console](https://www.xmediacloud.com/contact-us/) and go to the DRM settings page to obtain the UID, SecretID, and SecretKey.
4. On the [DRM management](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/live/config/drm) page of the CSS console, enter the information obtained.
