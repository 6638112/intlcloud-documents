LVB provides a convenient one-stop service for the online education industry. ## LCB

### Security surveillance

LCB pushes live streams in real time through a number of cameras from different angles, and supports real-time video playback. It works effectively in kitchens which need supervision of food production safety, real-time traffic monitoring, campus security and other use cases.

<img width="700px" src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/619ee324e482d5a99dbbb31a9fb6037d.png" >

### Scenic attractions and tours

Cameras are deployed in multiple scenic attractions in a region for 24/7 shooting. Leveraging the high concurrent push capability and video distribution capability of Tencent Cloud, LCB enables users to view real-time scenic attractions anytime, anywhere.

<img width="700px" src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/de3dc77fb8002c450ff81e724571b9a2.png" >

### Automobile 4S shop

With the help of cameras in the shop, an automobile 4S shop can identify customers and hot spots through YouTu boxes (edge terminals). The shop analyzes customer traffic hot spots through Tencent Cloud, making the shop more efficient. An enterprise can use Tencent Cloud APIs to synchronize customer events between its shops so that potential customers can be quickly identified when entering a shop. The APIs can also be used to synchronize shop data and push reminders to the sales staff in the shop.
