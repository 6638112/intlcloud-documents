## Applying for Trial License
We provide free trial license for basic edition SDK. The license is valid for 14 days and you can renew it for once; that is 28 days in total. You can apply for the license by the following three steps.

1. Log in to LVB Console, select **LVB SDK** > **[License](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/live/license)** in the left menu bar.

   ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/99435a29a00bde5ad866480738d45df4.png)

2. Click **Get License**, enter Android package name and iOS bundle ID. Enter “-” if you don’t have a package name or Bundle ID.

   ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/5f721dd4406fbc7fb8019591c771b467.png)

3. Click **Confirm** to create a trial license. You can view information of the license by clicking small triangle icon on the left side. Please note down the “Key” and “License URL” for initializing the SDK.

   ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/b4d1f52146cd25c595a45a3c56c4dff5.png)

4. The trial license is valid for 14 days after first application and you can renew it for once. Click **Edit** on the right of “Trial License” to go to the modification interface. Click **OK** to renew the license for another 14 days.

   ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/b9f30d4ca8a3349b2003d15008a85b83.png)

## Purchasing Official License

1. If you want to buy the official license of the basic edition SDK, please click **Contact Sales** in **Purchase Official License** window.

   ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/4fc8840a064c7ebb1ccbef999574fa47.png)

2. After you submit the ticket, soon our sales will contact you and help you purchase an official license.

   ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/107540d0d8d7e29177400a83689bd36d.png)

   Please check the bundle ID and package name again before clicking **Confirm**, and modify them if they are not the same as the information submitted in the application store. The information of the official license cannot be modified after submission.

3. The information of the official license will be recorded on your LVB Console after successful purchase. Click the small triangular icon on the left to view the license information. Please note down the “Key” and “License URL” for initializing the SDK.

   ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/d2dd4ee349a52b10c4609d655fd54bc8.png)

   If you have any questions, please click **Contact Sales**.
