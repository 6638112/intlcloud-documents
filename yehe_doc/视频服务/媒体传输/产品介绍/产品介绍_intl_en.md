Tencent Cloud MediaConnect (MDC) provides professional, stable, and high-quality video transfer services for global users. Leveraging the compute resources in Tencent Cloud's abundant AZs deployed around the globe and proprietary world-leading audiovisual technologies, MediaConnect implements stable and secure real-time transfer capabilities and comprehensively monitors the quality of video streams during the transfer, enabling video providers to quickly and stably transfer video streaming media.

## Multiprotocol Primary/Backup Inputs
MediaConnect can transfer streaming media over multiple protocols such as RTP, SRT, UDP, and RTMP. In addition, it automatically supports redundancy configuration by providing primary and backup input addresses for each flow to ensure the flow stability and reliability.

## Comprehensive Stream Quality Monitoring
MediaConnect offers detailed health reports on the running status of each flow and displays information of alerts in various types, making it easy for you to monitor the flow quality in real time.

## High Stability and Low Latency
MediaConnect can optimize the transfer path according to the destination address to provide stable live streaming transfer services across continents with a latency down to within 1 second.

## One-Flow-Multi-Node Transfer
MediaConnect supports the one-flow-multi-node transfer mechanism where multiple output nodes can be added for the same flow to transfer the flow to different regions.

## Support for Protocol Remuxing
MediaConnect supports remuxing from and to RTP, SRT, and RTMP streaming media protocols, which means that one flow can be remuxed into RTP, SRT, and RTMP streams at the same time.

## High Scalability
MediaConnect can be integrated with other Tencent Cloud services such as MediaLive, MediaPackage, LVB, and CDN to implement a full linkage of large-scale broadcast-grade one-stop media services.
