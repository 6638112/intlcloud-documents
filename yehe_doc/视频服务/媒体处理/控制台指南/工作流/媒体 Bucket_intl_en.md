## Overview
The **Buckets** page shows the source and output buckets that have been bound with your schemes.

## Details
1. Log in to [MPS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/mps) and select [Workflow > Buckets](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/mps/workflows/bucket) on the left sidebar. The page shows the **source buckets** and **output buckets** bound with your schemes under different tabs.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/68ccfa19fd29b4bd30b2eedd2446bce3.png)
2. Click **View files** in the operation column to view the files in a bucket.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/01bd68d7e482fcc70078972fe310c0f1.png)

>? This page shows only buckets bound with MPS schemes. To create buckets or perform other bucket operations, please go to the [COS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cos/bucket).
