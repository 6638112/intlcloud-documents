## Content Analysis Templates[](id:analyze)
1. MPS provides preset content analysis templates, which can be added directly to a scheme. You can also click **Create template** to customize your own content analysis templates.

<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th>Configuration Item</th>
<th>Description</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody><tr>
<td>Template Name</td>
<td>Max 64 characters; supports Chinese characters, letters, digits, spaces, underscores (_), hyphens (-), and periods (.).</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Content analysis configuration</td>
<td><strong>Intelligent labeling</strong>, <strong>intelligent categorization</strong>, <strong>intelligent thumbnail generation</strong>, and <strong>frame-specific labeling</strong></td>
</tr>
</tbody></table>

>? You can view **preset** content analysis templates in [Templates > Content Discovery Templates](https://console.intl.cloud.tencent.com/mps/templates/mls?tab=analysis) of the MPS console.

2. The templates created are displayed in the content analysis template list, where you can view the details of, edit, or delete a template.
