To enable you to better use MPS and configure required service capabilities more easily, MPS features, capabilities, and user interaction have been upgraded in several ways:

- **Comprehensive and visual product usage data**: You can view comprehensive data on the [Overview](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/mps) and [Usage Statistics](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/mps/statistics?tab=transcode/) pages.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/d5cfd19126059fd1b3d83039f2bc9762.png)
- **Improved task management and operation pages**: You can manually create a task on the [Create task](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/mps/tasks/create) page. You can also view task and subtask information and manage tasks on the [Task management](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/mps/tasks) page.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/4b30c7d3c08488b4665541faad04740a.png)
- **More template capabilities**: You can configure required capabilities more easily, and AV1 encoding and video enhancement template capabilities are now available on the [Template Management](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/mps/templates/avs) page.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/c6ef8cdc926702971a53f5c71fda6459.png)

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/71703a164385fff593ca0cd76a20e46b.png)
- **Cost-effective resource packs**. You can purchase resource packs on the [MPS purchase page](https://buy.cloud.tencent.com/mps). For billing details, see [Prepaid Resource Packs](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1041/48810).
