## Overview
You can rename a CVD instance in the CVD console.
 
## Directions
1. Log in to the [CVD console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cvd).
2. On the **Desktop List** page, select the target CVD instance and click **More** > **Rename** on the right.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/5b72063775e1c20234b6bbdc09a86384.png)
3. In the **Rename** pop-up window, enter the new CVD instance name and click **Confirm**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/a2e98c2fdb0e436675c9324558a82907.png)
>! The CVD instance name will be displayed to end users at the portal.
