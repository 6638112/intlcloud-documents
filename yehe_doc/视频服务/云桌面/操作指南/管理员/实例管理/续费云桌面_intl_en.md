## Overview
You can renew a monthly subscribed CVD instance or set auto-renewal for it.

1. Log in to the [CVD console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cvd).
2. On the **Desktop List** page, select the target CVD instance.
3. Click **Renew** in the **Operation** column on the right or at the top.
4. In the **Renew** pop-up window, select a renewal period and click **Confirm**.

## Directions
### Setting auto-renewal[](id:automatically)
1. Log in to the [CVD console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cvd).
2. On the **Desktop List** page, select the target CVD instance.
3. Click **More** > **Auto-renew** in the **Operation** column on the right.
4. In the **Auto-renew** pop-up window, select **Enable** and click **Confirm**.
