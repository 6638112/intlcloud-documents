## Shutting down a CVD instance
You can shut down a CVD instance in the CVD portal.
Click <img style="width:20px; max-width: inherit;" src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/da47f15d6310b6abbe004bafa0cc7c07.png" /> in the target CVD instance card and select **Shut down**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/53e212e9c5019f5699e348a25d9cebd5.png)
>! Shutting down the instance may lead to loss of unsaved data. Make sure that your data has been saved.

## Restarting a CVD instance
You can restart a CVD instance in the CVD portal.
Click <img style="width:20px; max-width: inherit;" src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/da47f15d6310b6abbe004bafa0cc7c07.png" /> in the target CVD instance card and select **Restart**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/f3157d82f0b15752e41a9056f7df71d8.png)
>! Restarting the instance may lead to loss of unsaved data. Make sure that your data has been saved.

## Renaming
You can rename a CVD instance in the CVD portal.
Click <img style="width:20px; max-width: inherit;" src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/da47f15d6310b6abbe004bafa0cc7c07.png" /> in the target CVD instance card and select **Rename**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/78179fe596c5912ad83772542495cb2e.png)
In the **Rename** pop-up window, enter the new CVD instance name and click **Confirm**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/570804ce216174e985307f799118a1f2.png)
