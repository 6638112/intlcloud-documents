## Process
1. As an end user, you will receive an activation email or SMS. Follow the instructions in the email or SMS to activate your account.

2. Log in to the CVD console to view the desktop list. For detailed directions, see [Logging in to the CVD Console](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1167/51920).

3. Select a desktop from the list to access. For detailed directions, see [Opening a Desktop](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1167/51921).
