## Purchasing an Official License[](id:step2)

Go to the [Purchase Page](https://buy.intl.cloud.tencent.com/vcube) and buy a Tencent Effect package that meets your project requirements. After obtaining the license, follow the steps below to bind the license to your application.

>! You can [apply for a free trial license](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1143/50806) and download the [S1 - 04 SDK](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1143/45376#setS) at [SDK Download](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1143/45377) to try out the Tencent Effect SDK.

## Binding a License
Go to the [console](https://console.tencentcloud.com/xmagic) to bind the license to your application. For detailed directions, see [Adding and Renewing a License](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1143/50266).

>! 
>- You bind a Tencent Effect license to an application by specifying the bundle ID/package name. Make sure you have the information before binding a license.
>- You cannot change the bundle ID/package name bound to an official license. Please double check before you bind a license.
