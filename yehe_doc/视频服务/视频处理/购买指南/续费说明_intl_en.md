## Daily Pay-As-You-Go[](id:p1)
If you use the [daily pay-as-you-go billing mode](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1041/33478#day), fees are charged based on your actual usage of MPS features. Please pay attention to your account balance and top up in a timely manner.
1. Log in to the Tencent Cloud console and click [Billing Center](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/account/recharge) in the top-right corner. Select **Payment Management > Payment** on the left sidebar.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/8a90294c5bbfd0a90a09fe6862ab31a4.png)

2. Enter the top-up amount, and click **Pay Now**.

## Monthly Pay-As-You-Go
For [monthly pay-as-you-go](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1041/33478#month), the unit prices specified in your contract with Tencent Cloud will apply. For more information, please contact your sales rep.

## Prepaid Resource Packs[](id:p2)
You can buy resource packs in the console.
1. Log in to the [MPS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/mps). On the overview page, click **Buy resource pack** to go to the [purchase page](https://buy.cloud.tencent.com/mps).
2. Select the resource packs you want to buy.

>!
>- You can buy multiple resource packs. The one that expires first will be used first.
>- Each resource pack has its own validity period. If you buy multiple packs, their validity periods will not be added up.
>- Each MPS resource pack can only deduct a specific type of MPS resources.
>- After all your resource packs are used up, you will be automatically switched to the [pay-as-you-go](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1041/33478) mode.
