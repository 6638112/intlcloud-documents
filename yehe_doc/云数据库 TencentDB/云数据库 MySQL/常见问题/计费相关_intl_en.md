### Are pay-as-you-go instances still charged while they are being idle?
Yes. If you stop using pay-as-you-go resources, please terminate them as soon as possible to avoid further fee incursion.


### How is backup capacity billed?
TencentDB for MySQL offers a certain amount of backup capacity free of charge based on the region. The capacity is equivalent to the sum of storage capacity of all high-availability edition and finance edition instances (including source and disaster recovery instances) in the region. 

Excessive backup capacity will be charged at 0.000127 USD/GB/hour in Chinese mainland. For prices outside Chinese mainland, please see [TencentDB for MySQL Price Calculator](https://buy.cloud.tencent.com/price/cdb/calculator).

To reduce backup costs, please see [Suggestions for Reducing Backup Costs](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/236/32344#.E5.87.8F.E5.B0.91.E5.A4.87.E4.BB.BD.E5.BC.80.E9.94.80.E5.BB.BA.E8.AE.AE).
