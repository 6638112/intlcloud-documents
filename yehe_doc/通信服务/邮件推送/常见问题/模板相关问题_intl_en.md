### How long does an email template review take?
After you submit an email template, we will finish the review within three business days.

### What are the criteria of email template reviews?
The criteria are as follows:
1. Email length: at least 160 characters.
2. Variables: the ratio of variable to text should not exceed 1:5.
3. Images: no more than 50 images.
4. Content: unhealthy content involving phishing, top-up, lucky draw, pornography, drugs, violence, gambling, etc. is prohibited.
