## Purchase limits for pay-as-you-go CVM instances
- Identity verification is required to purchase pay-as-you-go CVM instances. Users who are not verified cannot make the purchase.
  **Identity verification methods:** Tencent Cloud identity verification is divided into personal identity verification and enterprise identity verification. For more information, please see [Identity Verification Guide] (https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/3629).
  Pay-as-you-go CVM instances do **not support**:
 - Agents paying on your behalf
 - 5-day unconditional refunds
 - CVMs using shared cores
 - Bandwidth packages and monthly billing cycles
- The **purchase limit** of pay-as-you-go CVM instances for each user in each availability zone is between 30 and 60. Please see the CVM purchase page for the exact purchase limit.
