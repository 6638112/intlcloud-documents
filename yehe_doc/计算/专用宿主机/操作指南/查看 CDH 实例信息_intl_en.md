You can obtain CDH instance information through the console or API.

### Viewing CDH instance information on the console

1. Log in to the [CVM Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cvm/).

2. Choose **Dedicated Hosts** in the left sidebar.

3. On the **Dedicated Host** page, select a desired region. Then you can view information about all CDH instances in the selected region, including the AZ, model, total and remaining resources, and expiration date.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/75abff63caa003799977d919105fcb9a.png)

4. Click the ID of a CDH instance. On the details page displayed, you can view the detailed information of the CDH instance and information of dedicated CVMs on the CDH instance.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/3c8ad82eb0a6379ba1ee45a2c2930735.png)
