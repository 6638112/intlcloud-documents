## Product Purchase
Log in to the [purchase page of Tencent Cloud TKE](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/tke2/cluster?rid=1). When logging in to this page for the first time, you need to perform the following steps to complete service authorization before you can purchase TKE products.

## Service Authorization
>Ignore the following procedure if you have completed service authorization.
>
1. View information in the displayed "Service Authorization" dialog box, and click **Go to Cloud Access Management**.
2. On the "Role Management" page, read information related to the role.
3. Click **Agree to Authorize** to grant authorization. Now, you can go to the [purchase page of Tencent Cloud TKE](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/tke2/cluster?rid=1) to purchase related products.
