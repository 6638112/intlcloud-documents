1. Log in to the [VPC Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vpc/vpc?rid=1).
2. Click **Cloud Connect Network** on the left sidebar to enter the Cloud Connect Network management page.
3. In the list, click the ID of the CCN instance to be modified to enter the details page.
4. Click the **Bandwidth Management** tab to enter the details page where you can view the configured region outbound bandwidth limit.
5. (Optional) Adjust the bandwidth limit.
     1. Click **Change** on the right of **Speed limit mode**.
      ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/c66d120e44a69834f6f9ca8ee655d328.png)
     2. In the **Change speed limit mode** pop-up window, select from the drop-down list of **Speed limit mode**.
>!The original bandwidth limit setting will be deleted after the adjustment. The default bandwidth is 1 Gbps. You can [submit a ticket](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/workorder/category) to apply for a higher bandwidth.

![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/93232c58d1e626eaab54685923cc2009.png)

3. Click **OK**.
