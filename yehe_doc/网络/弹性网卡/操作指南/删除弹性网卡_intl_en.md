>
>- When an ENI is deleted, its associated private IPs, EIPs, and security groups are automatically unbound.
>- You can only delete ENIs that are not associated with any CVM.
>- The primary ENI is deleted when the CVM is deleted.
>
1. Log in to the [VPC Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vpc).
2. Choose **IP and ENI** -> **ENI** in the left sidebar to go to the ENI list page.
3. Locate the ENI you want to delete, and click **Delete** under its **Operation** column.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/15bb91ace6033e6e43f426625773f68c.png)
4. Click **OK** in the pop-up window.
