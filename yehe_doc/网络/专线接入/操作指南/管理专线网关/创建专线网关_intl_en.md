1. Log in to [Virtual Private Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vpc/vpc?rid=1).
2. Click **Direct Connect Gateway** in the left sidebar to enter to the management page.
3. Select a region and VPC, and then click **+ New**.
4. Fill in the relevant information in the pop-up window.
 - Name: the name of your direct connect gateway
 - Associate network: Virtual Private Cloud (VPC) or Cloud Connect Network (CCN)
 - Gateway type: standard or NAT (i.e., network address translation feature)
5. Click “OK” to complete the creation.
