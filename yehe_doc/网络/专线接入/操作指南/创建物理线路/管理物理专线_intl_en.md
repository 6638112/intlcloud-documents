After the connection is operating, you can view or delete the connection, add tags, or perform other operations on the console.

## Viewing the Connection
1. Log in to the [Direct Connect console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/dc/dc) and click **Connections** on the left sidebar.
2. Click the ID/Name of the connection that you want to view.
3. The **Basic info** displays the basic connection information, including the connection provider, port type, access point, bandwidth, and redundant connection.</br><img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/24ec0ecab4f1eed2b2ad386dff465f73.png" style="zoom:80%;" />
	
## Adding Tags
To easily locate and manage the connections under your account, perform the following steps to add tags to your connections.
1. Log in to the [Direct Connect console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/dc/dc) and click **Connections** on the left sidebar.
2. Select the connection to which a tag is added, and click **Edit Tags** in the **Operation** column.
3. Select a tag key and tag value in the corresponding drop-down list. You can also click **Manage Tags** to create your desired tags.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/1262d01ad4f8aa4733f252dd8003f59e.png)
4. Use a tag to locate the connection.
 1. Click the edit box in the top right of the **Connections** page, and select **Tags** in the drop-down list.
 <img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/4f6334a7c0a4e5e0a6555ac3005312a9.png" style="zoom:80%;" />
 2. Enter the tag in the edit box and click the magnifying glass icon.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/4a8101f8b8455b5a063992a3592ad18a.png)
5. Use a tag to manage the connections.
 1. Click ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/f22b6d803bb59529e530d140ee669a1c.png) in the top right of the **Connections** page.
 2. In the pop-up window, select the target tags, and click **OK**.
<img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/22e5aeb5c3ad340fb93a23cd09f0a0d5.png" style="zoom:80%;" /></br>
Then the tag key will appear in the first column of the connection list.
<img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/bd250accded3a435cbccdcad5479c262.png" style="zoom:80%;" />

## Deleting a Connection
You can cancel a connection when you no longer need it.
1. Log in to the [Direct Connect console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/dc/dc) and click **Connections** on the left sidebar.
2. Select the connection to be deleted, and click **Delete** in the **Operation** column.
3. In the pop-up window, click **Confirm Deletion** and **Confirm**.
>?After the cancellation application is submitted, the connection will be immediately deleted, and the calculation of monthly port rental fees will stop.
