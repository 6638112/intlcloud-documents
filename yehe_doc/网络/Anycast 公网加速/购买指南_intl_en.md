## Billing Description
AIA fees consist of two parts: acceleration traffic fee and IP resource occupation fee.
>?In this document, the Asia Pacific region covers Hong Kong (China), Japan, South Korea, Thailand, Singapore, and India. The North America region covers the United States. The Europe region covers Russia and Frankfurt.
### Acceleration traffic fees
The traffic generated by an Anycast EIP is called acceleration traffic, which is billed by bandwidth at a unit price higher than that of public network traffic. The total acceleration traffic under an account is billed by monthly 95th percentile of either the outbound or inbound traffic (whichever is higher) in USD/Mbps/month as shown below:

>?95th percentile: fees are billed by calendar month. In a calendar month, all the valid 5-minute bandwidth values are sorted in a descending order, and the top 5% are removed. The highest value of the remaining values is used as the 95th percentile of the monthly peak bandwidth.
>Assume that all the bandwidth value points in a month of 30 days are valid. Because one value point is generated every 5 minutes, 12 value points are generated every hour, and 8,640 (12 x 24 x 30 = 8,640) value points are generated every month. All the value points are sorted in a descending order, the top 5% (8,640 × 5% = 432) are removed, and the bandwidth value of the 433rd point will be billed.

### Anycast EIP resource occupation fees
As an EIP, if any Anycast EIP is not bound to an instance, resource occupation fees will be incurred. For more information, please see the “IP Resource Fee” section in [Elastic IP Billing](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/213/17156).

## Billing Examples
Suppose you purchased multiple Anycast EIPs under an account. The traffic generated by these Anycast EIPs in a month will be aggregated, and billed by 95th percentile of the monthly bandwidth at a price as shown below:

| **Server Region** | **Public Ingress/Egress Region** | **Bandwidth Usage (Mbps)** | **Price (USD/Mbps/Month)** |
| -------------- | ------------------ | --------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Asia Pacific           | North America               | 10                    | 18.86                          |
| Asia Pacific | Chinese mainland | 100 | 29.33 |
| Europe          | North America               | 200                   | 18.86                          |
| Asia Pacific           | Asia Pacific               | 300                   | 18.86                          |

Final fees = 10 * 18.86 + 100 * 29.33 + 200 * 18.86 + 300 * 18.86 = 12,551.60 USD
