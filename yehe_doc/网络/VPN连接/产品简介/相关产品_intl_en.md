For information about products related to VPN connections, see the table below:

| Product name | Description |
|---------|---------|
| [VPC](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/215) | VPN connection is used to connect customer IDC with a VPC through an encrypted tunnel over the public network. |
| [Anti-DDoS Pro](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1029) | Anti-DDoS Pro instance can be bound to a VPN gateway to defend against DDoS and CC attacks with high-bandwidth protection. |
| [Direct Connect](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/216) | If your business is sensitive to delay and jitter, we recommend that you access a VPC via Direct Connect. |
| [Route Table](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/215/31810) | You need to add routing policies for your VPN gateway in the route table associated with a subnet, so that network requests can reach the customer gateway through the VPN tunnel. |
|[CCN](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1003) | VPN gateway for CCN can be associated with CCN to establish an encrypted communication between the IDC and CCN. |
