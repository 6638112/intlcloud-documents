For information about the products related to VPN connections, see the table below:

| Product name | Description |
|---------|---------|
| [VPC](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/215) | A VPN connection is used to connect your customer IDC and a VPC through an encrypted tunnel over public networks. |
| [Anti-DDoS Pro](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1029) | You can bind an Anti-DDoS Pro instance to a VPN gateway to defend against DDoS and CC attacks based on ultra-large bandwidth. |
| [Direct Connect](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/216) | If your business is sensitive to delay and jitter, we recommend you access a VPC via Direct Connect. |
| [Route Table](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/215/31810) | You need to add routing policies for your VPN gateway in the route table associated with a subnet, so that network requests can be sent to the customer gateway through the VPN tunnel. |
|[CCN](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1003) | A VPN gateway for CCN can be associated with CCN, through which, IDC establishes an encrypted communication with CCN. |
