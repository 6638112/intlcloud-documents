
When multiple Anti-DDoS Pro instances are used, you can set "instance names" to identify and manage instances quickly.

## Prerequisites
You need to [purchase an Anti-DDoS Pro instance](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1029/36115) and set the protected object's IP first.

## Directions
### Method 1
1. Log in to the [new Anti-DDoS Pro Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/antiddos-native/package) and select **Anti-DDoS Pro Instance** on the left sidebar.
2. Click the "Edit" icon on the second row in the "ID/Name" column of the target instance and enter a name.
>The name can contain 1–20 characters of any type.
>
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/3964443c6222e822be7a0bfec69fbf9a.png)

### Method 2
1. Log in to the [new Anti-DDoS Pro Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/antiddos-native/package) and click **Anti-DDoS Pro Instance** on the left sidebar.
2. In the instance list below, click the ID of the target instance in the "ID/Name" column to enter its basic information page.
3. On the basic information page of the instance, click the "Edit" pencil icon on the right of the instance name and enter a name.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/55b5e4b1da90497fb430346ed948f87f.png)
>The name can contain 1–20 characters of any type.
