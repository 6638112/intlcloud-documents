

## Prerequisites
You need to [purchase an Anti-DDoS Pro instance](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1029/36115) and set the protected object first.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [new Anti-DDoS Pro Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/antiddos-native/package) and select **Protection Configuration** on the left sidebar.
2. Select an Anti-DDoS Pro instance ID in the list on the left, such as "bgp-0000008o", and click **IP Port Protection** at the top on the right.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/1482525c1a2f880784e26b87b0548189.png)
3. Click **Set** in the "Exceptional Connection Protection" block on the right to enter the exceptional connection protection list.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/ecc1f7ff8d0eecddee60406014403ddb.png)
4. Click **Create** to create an exceptional connection protection rule.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/dc3cc8a68a45a1a5c343b04af4a8eb04.png)
5. After the creation is completed, an exceptional connection protection rule will be added to the exceptional connection protection list. You can modify the rule by clicking **Configure** in the "Operation" column on the right.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/85bb7ad9670db2a2dacd4cb1046346b6.png)
