

## Prerequisites
You need to [purchase an Anti-DDoS Pro instance](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1029/36115) and set the protected object first.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [new Anti-DDoS Pro Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/antiddos-native/package) and click **Protection Configuration** on the left sidebar.
2. Select an Anti-DDoS Pro instance ID in the list on the left, such as "bgp-000000co", and select **Domain Name Protection** at the top on the right.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/7c20e517d287e64f2b1edbc6a4c2648d.png)
3. Click **Set** in the "Targeted Protection" block on the right to enter the targeted protection rule list.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/e627637cce96bfc6bf65048a6b70c6a0.png)
4. Click **Create** to create a targeted protection rule, fill in relevant fields, and click **OK**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/6cd2b642946d053123a03387c7ab3cc5.png)
5. After the creation is completed, a targeted protection rule will be added to the targeted protection list. You can modify the rule by clicking **Configure** in the "Operation" column on the right.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/7b18c6cf421edabaae5e65efbeaa7f43.png)
