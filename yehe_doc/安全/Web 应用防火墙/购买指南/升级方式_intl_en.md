## WAF Plan Upgrade Method
1.	Log in to the [WAF console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/guanjia/tea-overview) and select **Asset center** > **Instance management** on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Instance management** page, select the target instance and click **Upgrade**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/9dcafae948eb2c316c06762827670d8b.png)


## Extra Domain Pack Upgrade Method
1. On the [Instance management page](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/guanjia/tea-instance-new), select the target instance and click **More** > **Domain pack**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/7045803af992276cf107b3079aa1f6f3.png)
2. In the **Extra domain pack** pop-up window, select the quantity and click **Buy now**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/29ee314aa652b9a9cd5a312e6db4bdae.png)

## Extra QPS Pack Upgrade Method
1. On the [Instance management page](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/guanjia/tea-instance-new), select the target instance and click **More** > **Extra QPS pack**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/6fd5c6bc7e4befd5a548d3ac29181811.png)
2. In the **Extra QPS pack** pop-up window, select the quantity and click **Buy now**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/0633c5914075da2e99cb6ae31bbe14fa.png)

## Bot and Application Security Upgrade Method
1. On the [Instance management page](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/guanjia/tea-instance-new), select the target instance and click **More** > **Bot and application security**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/cf07e4eb47a6e273e516cc08c832d8a2.png)
2. In the **Bot and application security protection** pop-up window, select the target service and click **Buy now**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/51b343d84bb768fb0c177029bf189007.png)

## Security Log Pack Upgrade Method
1. In the **Quota** section on the [Instance management page](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/guanjia/tea-instance-new), click **Expand log pack**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/93bce3ac07e1d197963d9417c373e7a3.png)
2. In the **Expand log pack** pop-up window, select the quantity and click **Buy now**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/4da4f83f4e03068c1d420ee953fa0461.png)

## Instance Details Upgrade
1. On the [Instance management page](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/guanjia/tea-instance-new), select the target instance and click the **instance ID**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/4a8e671819e0c30459c1f358e547aef2.png)
2. On the **Instance details** page, upgrade resources as needed.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/7a01b2a28d0c9fa5513acf3a13c810c6.png)
