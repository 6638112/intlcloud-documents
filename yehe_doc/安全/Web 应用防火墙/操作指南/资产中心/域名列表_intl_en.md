## Scenarios
This document instructs you to manage your domain names in the WAF console.

## Directions
### Adding and viewing a domain name
1. Log in to the [WAF console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/guanjia/tea-overview) and select **Asset Center** > **Domain Name List** on the left sidebar.
2. On the page displayed, click **Add domain name**.
3. On the pop-up page, configure the required parameters and click **OK**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/a466032fec1427992754abf35c9adc31.png)
4. On the domain name list page, click a domain name you want to view. The basic details of the domain name will be displayed.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/6076834973ddd60253d750223d6e4ab3.png)


### Toggling on protection switch
1. On the domain name list page, click the WAF switch ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/94b83aa30f6475b240f0fd3ecfd970ea.png). A window will pop up to ask you to confirm your operation.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/cc49a5f7438cd0360725dfb6c24b3ff8.png)
2. In the pop-up window, click **OK**. After the switch is on, WAF protection will take effect based on your custom policies and defense settings.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/395ca462c445a7ee01954c9fbbfd3cf9.png)
3. On the domain name list page, click the access logging switch ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/94b83aa30f6475b240f0fd3ecfd970ea.png). A window will pop up to ask you to confirm your operation.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/a85ef8d8c4e7bf7655d79dee7fe2aa76.png)
4. In the pop-up window, click **OK**. When the switch is on, access traffic of the domain name will be recorded.

### Editing a domain name
1. On the domain name list page, select the target domain name and click **Edit**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/54ed663a6c1ed1facd07c268da12e9ae.png)
2. On the page displayed, modify the server configuration, proxy, and origin address, and click **OK** to save your changes.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/c7efe736e3d5ffdfa1f291f6726680e8.png)

### Deleting a domain name
1. On the domain name list page, select a domain name to delete and click **Delete** on the right.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/8a57757fe0a8540d419e8daaf22ccca4.png)
2. In the pop-up window, click **OK** to delete the domain name.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/7248782f730ed6a7882a26a6f0dd3449.png)
