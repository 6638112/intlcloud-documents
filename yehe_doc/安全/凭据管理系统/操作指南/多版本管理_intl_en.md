## Overview
SSM allows you to manage multiple versions of secrets. You can leverage this feature to rotate secrets at the application layer in beta tests.
## Directions
1. Log in to the [SSM Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ssm) and click **Credential List** in the left sidebar. You can switch between regions from the upper left corner of the page to find the secret to which you want to add a version. You can click the name of the secret to go to the **Basic Information** page.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/880bd05193254d4fb6181aca39cf2970.png)
2. In the **Credential Management** area, click **Add** to go to the **Add Credential Information** page.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/c30d4b4b35a2aff0f434cfa8fa68ae7f.png)
3. On the **Add Credential Information** page, enter the credential version and credential content, and then click **Add**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/fe416b3f1ce3839e60d15031c38c91f3.png)
4. If you want to delete an added secret, you can click **Delete** in the **Operation** column on the right of the unwanted version, and then click **Delete** to confirm the deletion.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/0f8f55017e55757337cb8ebd583097a0.png)

>!Each secret can have a maximum of 10 versions at the same time.
