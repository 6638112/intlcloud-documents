This document describes how to download your private key on the SSM console.

## Prerequisite
You have created [an SSH key secret](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1078/42683). 

## Directions
1. Log in to the [SSM Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ssm) and click **CVM SSH Key** on the left sidebar.
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/065475881ba4b315348c9264cf6ca161.png)
2. On the **CVM SSH Key** page, click the drop-down list in the top left corner to select a region.
   ![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/9489836502937f07a2ff1bb3ca1bf33a.png)
3. Click the search box, select a filter (**Tag** or **Secret Name**) from the list, and enter keywords to get results.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/32072ac7a79d64de36fc31aa014a4271.png)
4. Locate the secret you want to check, and click the secret itself to enter the secret details page.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/674cd635eb0b3b2d16f74a4dfb6af5d4.png)
5. This page displays the SSH key information, including the name and ID of the private key and the public key content. On the right of the private key content, click **Download**.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/363d33d882f6718b197115289869cb9f.png)
