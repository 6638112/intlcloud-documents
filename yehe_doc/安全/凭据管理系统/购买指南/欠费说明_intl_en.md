### Arrears status
When your account falls into arrears, notifications for service suspension will be sent to your Tencent Cloud account creators and all collaborators through email and SMS on the day of and after expiration.
### Suspension status
Your SSM service will be suspended 24 hours after your account falls into arrears. At this time, your access to SSM will be denied, but your secret data will be retained. After the service is suspended, secret storage and API calls will not incur fees.
### Resource repossession
Secret resources will be released 168 hours (7 days) after account suspension. To use your secrets, please top up and renew your account immediately after it falls into arrears.
