### How is AMS billed?

AMS is postpaid by the volume of recognized audio. For billing mode details, see [Purchase Guide](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1139/44941).

### How do I top up my account?

To top up, log in to your [Tencent Cloud account](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/), click **Bills** > **Top-up Now** in the top-right corner, or click **Top-Up** in [Billing Center](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/expense) to enter the **Top-up** page.

### How do I get a refund?

For refund, [submit a ticket](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/workorder/category) or contact your sales or aftersales rep.
