## Civic Service
CIAM helps build a multi-level account mutual trust system, so people don't need to remember complicated accounts and passwords and can complete multiple business transactions upon one authentication, which improves the business processing efficiency and satisfaction of government services. It has helped tens of millions of users in many cities get business transactions done more quickly.

## Travel and Mobility
CIAM interconnects all application channels of a scenic area's platform to help tourists smoothly access various scenic spots, purchase local specialty products online, and check in at hotels with one QR code, delivering a seamless and enriched travel experience.

## Retail Promotion
CIAM offers MFA and malicious behavior identification capabilities to help you block malicious registration and bargain hunting and thus prevent business losses. It has helped hundreds of customers avoid tens of millions of CNY in economic losses.
