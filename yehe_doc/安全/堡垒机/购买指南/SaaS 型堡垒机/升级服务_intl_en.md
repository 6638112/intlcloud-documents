## Overview
This document describes how to upgrade the BH service when the currently supported assets become insufficient.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [BH console](https://console.intl.cloud.tencent.com/bh).
2. On the left sidebar, select **Activate service**.
3. On the **Activate service** page, select **Upgrade** on the right of the target service.
4. In the **Upgrade service** pop-up window, select the target specification.
5.	 After confirming the amount, click **OK** to submit the order. After the payment is made, the upgrade is successful.
