## Overview
This document describes how to renew the BH service that will expire soon.
## Directions
1. Log in to the [BH console](https://console.intl.cloud.tencent.com/bh).
2. On the left sidebar, select **Activate service**.
3. On the **Activate service** page, select **Renew** on the right of the target service.
4. In the **Renew service** pop-up window, select the renewal period.
5. After confirming the amount, click **OK** to submit the renewal order. After the payment is made, the renewal is successful.
