This document describes how to purchase SaaS BH.
## Prerequisites
Before purchasing BH, you need to [sign up for a Tencent Cloud account](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/17985) and complete [identity verification](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/3629).

## Directions
1. Go to the [BH purchase page](https://buy.intl.cloud.tencent.com/bh), select the package, specification, bandwidth, and validity period as needed, and click **Buy now**.
2. After confirming the order, you can pay with a credit card.
3. After making the payment, you can log in to the [BH console](https://console.intl.cloud.tencent.com/bh) for management.
