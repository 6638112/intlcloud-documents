## Expiration Alert
From seven days before your PTS service expires, the system will send alerts for expiration to your Tencent Cloud account creator, global resource collaborators, and financial collaborators by email and SMS.

## Repossession Mechanism
The PTS service is valid for one calendar year, and from seven days before it expires, the system will send alerts for expiration to you. If it is not renewed after expiration, it will be released within about 24 hours of expiration and become unavailable.
