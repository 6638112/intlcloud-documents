## Retail Membership Management

CIAM helps you build a unified user account risk management system to better manage users and conduct targeted marketing. You can also leverage Tencent's risk management capabilities to identify signup by malicious users and bargain hunters and avoid potential business losses.

## Travel and Mobility

CIAM interconnects all applications of a scenic area's platform to help tourists smoothly access various scenic spots, purchase local specialty products online, and check in at hotels with one tourist account, delivering a seamless and enriched travel experience.

## Healthcare

CIAM helps create a unified user account management system, with multiple identity options such as doctor and patient. In this way, hospitals can better manage patient information, patients can use services provided by hospitals more conveniently.
