Anti-DDoS Pro allows you to limit traffic rate for business IPs and ports.
## Prerequisites
You have successfully purchased an [Anti-DDoS Pro](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1029/36115) instance and set the protected target.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [Anti-DDoS Pro Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/antiddos-native/package) and click **Configurations** on the left sidebar.
2. Select an Anti-DDoS Pro instance ID in the list on the left, such as "bgp-00xxxxxx".
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/ec487cf32a98c863a461048bbe42faaf.png)
3. Click **Set** in the **IP/Port Speed Limit** section on the right.
 ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/da09cb5f06de8d5f9646f9f84ff16f3f.png)
4. Click **Create** to create an IP/port rate limiting rule.
5. In the pop-up window, select a protocol, port and rate limiting mode, enter a rate limiting threshold, and click **OK**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/f2139b4be179346b825f94918af73dd2.png)
6. Now the new rule is added to the list. You can click **Configuration** on the right of the rule to modify it.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/03e0d7f76e1ed34398b5f6041e6c8ce0.png)
