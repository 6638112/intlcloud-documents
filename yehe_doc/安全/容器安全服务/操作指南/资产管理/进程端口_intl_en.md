This document describes the processes and ports feature and how to view the process and port lists.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/d424cdcf40340e1859c044cb6530a68f.png)

## Viewing the Process Module
The process module displays the total number of processes.
### Filtering processes
1. Log in to the [TCSS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/tcss) and click **Asset Management** on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Asset Management** page, click **Processes** to enter the process list page.
<img src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/1ae6af83120ad1ffe294559c5c2bf895.png" style="zoom:80%;" />
3. On the process list page, click the search box and search for processes by keyword such as initiator, server name, and process name.
<img src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/0770fdff05fcb0988ba4edf8307e0eb9.png" style="zoom:80%;" />

### Viewing the list of containers
1. Log in to the [TCSS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/tcss) and click **Asset Management** on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Asset Management** page, click **Processes** to enter the process list page.
<img src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/be2cba3665df5ea60d2238bbb8d2cf58.png" style="zoom:80%;" />
3. On the process list page, click a **Server IP** to pop up the drawer on the right, which displays the server details, including the basic server information, Docker information, and the numbers of images and containers.
>?In the drawer, click the number to view the numbers of images and containers on the server.
>![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/fecdcce88d85f1c710e0b749635d1d69.png)

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/002737b4ce93a1286998853d2b0e6692.png)

### Custom list management
1. Log in to the [TCSS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/tcss) and click **Asset Management** on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Asset Management** page, click **Processes** to enter the process list page.
<img src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/3af5d0e0111a1705c47e5e34e53ebc2c.png" style="zoom:80%;" />
3. On the process list page, click ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/d42b27540eef9bf90a9e30f96b500bf3.png) to pop up the **Custom List Management** window.
3. In the pop-up window, select the target type and click **OK**.
<img src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/c8adc9e1f351a24f63b33b8fe2822b99.png" style="zoom:80%;" />

## Viewing the Port Module
The port module displays the total number of ports.
### Filtering ports
1. Log in to the [TCSS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/tcss) and click **Asset Management** on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Asset Management** page, click **Ports** to enter the port list page.
<img src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/8525372071cc895fa1a721c152f31c4b.png" style="zoom:80%;" />
3. On the port list page, click the search box and search for ports by keyword such as server IP, process name, or host port.
![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/acb6440226add1c47b8e938418ecf72c.png)

### Viewing the list of ports
1. Log in to the [TCSS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/tcss) and click **Asset Management** on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Asset Management** page, click **Ports** to enter the port list page.
<img src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/8eb366ac967b287d9a9003f5ea238a6b.png" style="zoom:80%;" />
3. On the port list page, click a **Server IP** to pop up the drawer on the right, which displays the server details, including the basic server information, Docker information, and the numbers of images and containers.
>?In the drawer, click the number to view the numbers of images and containers on the server.
>![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/dabf6dafae2018dd489b7533aee7b924.png)

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/17503a29b2adac0081082893ea560393.png)

### Custom list management
1. Log in to the [TCSS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/tcss) and click **Asset Management** on the left sidebar.
2. On the **Asset Management** page, click **Ports** to enter the port list page.
<img src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/1eb616ec62b87e2846a53cfed03b8cdd.png" style="zoom:80%;" />
3. On the port list page, click ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/d42b27540eef9bf90a9e30f96b500bf3.png) to pop up the **Custom List Management** window.
3. In the pop-up window, select the target type and click **OK**.
<img src="https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/149d3e615ff19766f53fa45980dddca2.png" style="zoom:80%;" />
