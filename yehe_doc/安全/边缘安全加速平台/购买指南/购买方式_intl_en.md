## Prerequisite
[Sign up for a Tencent Cloud account](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/17985).

## Standard Plan Activation
1. Log in to the [EdgeOne console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/edgeone) and click **Site Overview** on the left sidebar.
2. On the page that appears, click **Add site** in the top-right corner.
3. Enter the target site. For more details, see [Adding Sites](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1145/45966).
4. Choose your plan. To subscribe to an Enterprise plan, click **Get a quote** or [contact us](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/contact-us). For the Standard plan, **Bot management** is provided as an additional feature, which increases the subscription fee. You can select it as needed.
>? EdgeOne offers pay-as-you-go subscription plans. The monthly fee is calculated and deducted on the first day of the next month.

## Enterprise Plan Activation (or Trial)

To activate your Enterprise plan or start your Enterprise plan trial, submit your application in the following way:

- Fill in an [application form](https://questionnaire-1312684008.cos.ap-singapore.myqcloud.com/EO-questionnaire.pdf) and send it to the email address eo_op@tencent.com.

We will process your application as soon as possible when it's submitted.
