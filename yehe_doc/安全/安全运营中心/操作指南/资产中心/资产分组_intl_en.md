
Asset Center provides the asset group feature to facilitate the security control over assets. You can group assets, view assets by group, generate security reports by asset group, or filter assets with the same attributes by asset group.
## Directions
1. Log on to the [Security Operation Center Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ssav2/assets/group). In the left navigation pane, click **Asset Center** to go to the Asset Group page.
2. On the Asset Group page, you can add, edit, and delete groups.
  - **Add a group**
	 1. In the upper-left corner of the Asset Group page, click **Add Group**. An "Add Group" pop-up window appears.
	![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/19778900cdf99f92185f168bc622c836.png)
	 2. In the "Add Group" pop-up window, fill in the name and description of the asset group, select "Add assets manually" or "Batch add assets", and then click **OK**.
>? You can add up to 5 groups to one asset.

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/7343578b139819bad1b52e154ecc8315.png)	
 - **Edit a group**
	 1. In the operation pane to the right of the asset group to edit, click **Edit** to go to the Edit Group page.
	![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/7ca2e4196c2e1e7e4a4e5119462d8b68.png)
	 2. On the Edit Group page, you can modify the name and description of an asset group and edit the asset, and click **OK** after the modification is completed.
	![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/3764ace7626e71bfc987a3c40320b03f.png)
 - **Delete a group**
	You can delete unwanted asset groups. Click **Delete** in the operation pane to the right of the asset group to delete, and then click **OK** in the "Delete Group" pop-up window that appears.
 ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/0aebbb3183daf3499eba202ab6199dc1.png)
>! Before deleting an asset group, check whether the asset group is referenced by the [Report Task](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ssav2/report/task). If referenced, use caution when deleting it.
