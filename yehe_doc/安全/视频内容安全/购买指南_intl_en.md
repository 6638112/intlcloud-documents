## Billing Overview

Video Moderation System (VM) adopts the daily pay-as-you-go mode. To get the quotation or activate the service, please [contact us](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/contact-us). 


## Overdue Payment Policy

VM is daily pay-as-you-go by the actual usage and will be automatically suspended if your Tencent Cloud account has overdue payments.
