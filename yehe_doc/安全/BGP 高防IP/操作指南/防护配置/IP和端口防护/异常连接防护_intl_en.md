


## Prerequisites
You need to [purchase an Anti-DDoS Advanced instance](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/297/15483) and set the protected object first.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [new Anti-DDoS Advanced Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/antiddos-advanced/package) and click **Protection Configuration** on the left sidebar.
2. Select the ID or port of an Anti-DDoS Advanced instance in the list on the left, such as "212.64.xx.xx bgpip-000002je" or "212.64.xx.xx bgpip-000002je" > "TCP:8000".
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/f7e8a12e1d056a4ef00ca0741685974f.png)
3. Click **Set** in the "Exceptional Connection Protection" block on the right to enter the exceptional connection protection list.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/5998491511d937b5d3e5c12635d331a0.png)
4. Click **Create** to create an exceptional connection protection rule.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/5998491511d937b5d3e5c12635d331a0.png)
5. After the creation is completed, an exceptional connection protection rule will be added to the exceptional connection protection list. You can modify the rule by clicking **Configure** in the "Operation" column on the right.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/4774d8db4b83a4c913f5db560243ce5b.png)
