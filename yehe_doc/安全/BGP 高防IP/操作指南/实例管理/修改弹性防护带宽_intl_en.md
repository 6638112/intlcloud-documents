
Elastic protection bandwidth refers to the capability that Anti-DDoS can provide to defend against attack traffic. If the attack traffic exceeds the protection bandwidth, the attacked IPs will be blocked.

## Prerequisites
You need to [purchase an Anti-DDoS Advanced instance](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/297/15483) first.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [new Anti-DDoS Advanced Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/antiddos-advanced/package) and click **Instance List** on the left sidebar.
2. Click the "Edit" icon next to the elastic protection bandwidth in the "Protection Specification" column of the target Anti-DDoS Advanced instance.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/5fa0b062316514e217dad08f65f1691b.png)
3. In the elastic protection settings window that pops up, select the elastic protection bandwidth based on your actual protection needs.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/08dcb5f593e30e51c430fbebeb37825e.png)
4. After completing the selection, click **OK**.
