

## Directions
1. Log in to the [new Anti-DDoS Advanced Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/antiddos-advanced/access/l7) and click **Business Connection** on the left sidebar. On the business connection page, click **Domain Name Connection** to enter the corresponding management page.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/01495e735af641b8a8f0af4d36c1ef18.png)
2. On the "Domain Name Connection" tab, click **Add Domain Name**. In the pop-up window for creating a layer-7 forwarding rule, fill in relevant fields.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/21d74e651807145a4a67c9a538f769db.png)
3. After completing the settings, click **OK**.
