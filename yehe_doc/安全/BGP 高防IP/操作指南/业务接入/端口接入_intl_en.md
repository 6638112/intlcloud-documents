

## Directions
1. Log in to the [new Anti-DDoS Advanced Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/antiddos-advanced/access/l4) and click **Business Connection** on the left sidebar. On the business connection page, click **Port Connection** to enter the corresponding management page.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/9c5c4218444ab587ce0cc7119a1f64a6.png)
2. On the "Port Connection" tab, click **Add Rule**. In the pop-up window for creating a layer-4 forwarding rule, fill in relevant fields.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/d3f48270f3ae919cc07a6f0605535370.png)
2. After completing the settings, click **OK**.
