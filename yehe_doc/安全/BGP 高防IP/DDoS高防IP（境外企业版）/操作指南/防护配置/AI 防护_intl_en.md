Anti-DDoS allows you to enable AI protection for powerful defense effect. With AI protection enabled, Anti-DDoS will learn connection baselines and traffic features using algorithms, auto-tune its cleansing policies, and detect and block 4-layer CC attacks.

## Prerequisites
You have purchased an Anti-DDoS Advanced (Global Enterprise Edition) instance and set your target to protect.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [Anti-DDoS Advanced (Global Enterprise Edition) Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/ddos-basic) and select **Anti-DDoS Advanced** -> **Configurations** on the left sidebar.
2. Select an Anti-DDoS Advanced instance ID in the list on the left, such as "xxx.xx.xx.xx bgpip-000003n2".
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/d6586694f3fc6b1cbc6099b6b1498c38.png)
3. Click **Set** in the **AI Protection** section to enter the AI protection page.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/e06a0e3066819c70bf24a5207e29d299.png)
4. Click **Create** to create an AI protection rule.
5. In the pop-up window, enable AI protection and click **OK**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/e4ae25644fcaf4b772cccc4a1d60b5f4.png)
6. Now the new AI protection rule is added to the list. You can click **Configuration** on the right of the rule to modify it.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/a1af33e5f92279f704ef6019e22c7e92.png)
