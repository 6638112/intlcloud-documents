### How is TMS billed?

TMS is postpaid. For pricing details, consult your sales rep.

### Is there a monthly subscription available?

No.

### How do I top up my account?

To top up, log in to your [Tencent Cloud account](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/), click **Bills** > **Top-up Now** in the top-right corner, or click **Top-Up** in [Billing Center](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/expense) to enter the **Top-up** page.
