## TKE

Cluster management fees and cloud resource fees are charged based on the actual usage of managed clusters with different specifications. For the specific prices of TKE, see [TKE Billing Overview](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/zh/document/product/457/45157).


## EKS

EKS is billed by the CPU, GPU, and memory resources requested by workloads and the running time of workloads. It is suitable for different needs in different use cases. For the specific prices of EKS, see [Billing Overview](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/457/34054).
