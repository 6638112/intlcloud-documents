## TKE

Cluster management fees and cloud resource fees are charged based on the actual usage of managed clusters with different specifications. For TKE billing details, see [TKE Billing Overview](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/457/45157).


## EKS

EKS is billed by the CPU, GPU, and memory resources requested by workloads and the running time of workloads. It is suitable for different needs in different use cases. For the specific prices of EKS, see [Billing Overview](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/457/34054).


## TKE Edge

TKE Edge charges **cluster management fees** for user-created managed cluster's control plane and **node management fees** for user-accessed compute nodes in edge clusters.
