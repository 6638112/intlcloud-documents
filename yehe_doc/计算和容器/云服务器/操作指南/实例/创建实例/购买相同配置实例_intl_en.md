## Overview
If you want to purchase again an instance with the same configurations, you can use the **Purchase with same configurations** feature on the CVM Console to save time and improve scale-out efficiency.

## Directions

1. Log in to the [CVM Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cvm/instance/index?rid=19).
2. Select a region at the top of the **Instances** page.
3. Under the **Operation** column of the instance, click **More** -> **Purchase with same configurations**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/990ca94e95a27a66b46a7ee5d20e296a.png)
4. Enter the quantity of CVMs you want to purchase and check the other automatically selected configurations.
You can adjust the parameter configurations based on your actual needs.

5. Click **Purchase** and complete the payment.
