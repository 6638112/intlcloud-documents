[Tencent Cloud Bandwidth Package (BWP)](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/684) is a multi-IP aggregated billing method that significantly reduces Internet access costs. When public network instances have traffic peaks at different times, you can use BWP for the aggregated bandwidth billing to save costs. BWP supports monthly top 5 and monthly 95th percentile billing for different use cases. BWP helps you reduce Internet access costs and increase your cost efficiency.

> ?BWP is currently in beta. To try it out, please submit an application.
