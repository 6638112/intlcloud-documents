The following describes how to purchase NAT gateways in the NAT Gateway console.
1. Log in to the [**NAT Gateway console**](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vpc/nat?fromNav).
2. Select a region and VPC, and then click **Create**.
3. In the **Create NAT Gateway** page, specify the **Gateway Name**, **Gateway Type**, **Network** and **Elastic IP**. Click **Create**.
