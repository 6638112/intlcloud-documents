

If the acceleration domain name that you are connecting meets one of the following conditions, you need to verify your domain name ownership:

- The domain name is being connected for the first time.
- The domain name has been connected by another user.
- The domain name is a wildcard domain name.

## Directions

1. Click **Verification Method** to get the DNS record information for DNS verification. Do not close this page before verification is completed.

2. If your DNS service provider is Tencent Cloud, go to the [DNSPod console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cns), find the corresponding domain name, click **Record**, add a TXT record, and enter `_cdnauth` as its host record.

3. Wait for the TXT record to take effect and then click **Verify**.
