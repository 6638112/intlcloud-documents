>!Disabling a route may affect running services. Therefore, make sure that no data is being forwarded through this route before performing this operation.
>

## Directions
1. Log in to the [CCN console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vpc/ccn) and enter the CCN management page.
2. In the CCN list, click the **ID/Name** of the CCN instance for which to disable a route to enter the details page.
3. On the **Route Table** tab, find the row of the routing policy to be disabled and toggle off the switch in the **Enable routing** column.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/616bb4c9992e8549a7398db8ade1714c.png)
4. In the pop-up window, click **OK**.
