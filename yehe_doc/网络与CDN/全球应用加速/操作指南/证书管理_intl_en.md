## Adding a Certificate
1. Log in to the [GAAP console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/gaap), enter the **Certificate Management** page and click **Add**.
2. Enter the certificate information.
 ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/20e464cd77fa2b4853527b426e0e8a6f.png)
- **Certificate Name**: user-defined name of the certificate.
- **Certificate Type**: supports basic authentication configurations, client CA certificate, server SSL certificate, origin CA certificate, and connection SSL certificate, of which a key is required for server SSL certificates and connection SSL certificates, and can be purchased in Tencent Cloudâ€™s **SSL Certificate Management**.
- **Certificate Content**: supports certificate content in PEM format.
- **Key Content**: supports key content in PEM format.

## Certificate Details
Enter the **Certificate Management** page, and click **ID/Name** or **Details** of a certificate you want to check.

## Deleting a Certificate
Enter the **Certificate Management** page and click **Delete** of a certificate you want to remove. Then click **OK** on the pop-up window.
