## Prerequisites

-	You have verified your enterprise identity for your Tencent Cloud account and activated [GAAP](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/products/gaap).
-	If you haven't activated GAAP, click [here](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/apply/p/k47b8r3qt2), fill out the product application form, and submit it for approval. After the application is approved, you can log in to the [GAAP console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/gaap) to use it.
-	If you have already activated GAAP but cannot find the shared acceleration feature in the [GAAP console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/gaap), [contact us](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/608/42346) for assistance.
