>?To ensure that the services of tunnel users work properly, users can only change the bandwidth of tunnels but not delete tunnels.
>

1. Log in to the [Direct Connect console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vpc/dcConn).
2. On the left sidebar, click **Dedicated Tunnels** to go to the management page.
3. In the list, locate the dedicated tunnel to delete, choose **More** -> **Delete**, and click **OK**.
4. You can create a dedicated tunnel with the same VLAN ID only after the deletion operation is completed.
