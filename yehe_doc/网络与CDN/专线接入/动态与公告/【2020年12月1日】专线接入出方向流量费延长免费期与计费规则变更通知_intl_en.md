This notification is to inform you about the changes of Direct Connect free quota and billing rules.
1. The free trial period of outbound traffic is extended to December 1, 2022.
2. The billable outbound traffic changes from the traffic going out over the port shared by partners to the traffic going out from the Direct Connect gateway. For more information, see [Billing and Pricing](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/216/543).
