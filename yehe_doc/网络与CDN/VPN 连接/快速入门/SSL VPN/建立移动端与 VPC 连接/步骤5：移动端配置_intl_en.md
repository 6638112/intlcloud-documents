Now, you need to configure the SSL VPN client certificate on the mobile client.

## Prerequisite
Download the [OpenVPN](https://github.com/OpenVPN) and install it to your mobile client.

## Directions
1. Download the SSL VPN client certificate created in [Step 3](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1037/43901).
2. Upload the downloaded SSL VPN client certificate to the OpenVPN of your mobile client.
