## IPSec VPN Pricing[](id:IPSecdingjia)
>? VPN tunnel and customer gateway are **free of charge**.
>

### VPN Gateway Fee
VPN gateway is your target gateway on Tencent Cloud side. The traffic flowing through the VPN gateway incurs fees. The gateway fee is billed by traffic.
>?The traffic here refers to the outbound traffic of the VPN gateway, which is also called the downstream traffic.

### Bill-by-traffic
Bill-by-traffic is a pay-as-you-go billing mode and involves two parts of fee: **traffic fee** generated by public network access and **gateway fee** (billed on an hourly basis, and the billing less than 1 hour is calculated as an hour).

#### Billing example
Assume a user purchases 50 Mbps VPN gateway in Beijing with the bill-by-traffic option and uses a total of 5 GB traffic between 07:00:00-07:59:59, then at 08:00:00, the user will be required to pay the following fees:
+ Traffic fee: The traffic price is 0.12 USD/GB in Beijing. Traffic fee = traffic price × used traffic = 0.12 USD/GB × 5 GB = 0.6 USD
+ Gateway fee: The price of 50 Mbps VPN gateway in Beijing region is 0.074 USD/hr. Gateway fee = gateway price × duration = 0.074 USD/hr × 1 hour = 0.074 USD

Total fee = traffic fee + gateway fee = 0.6 USD + 0.074 USD = 0.674 USD

#### Billing example
Assume a user purchases 50 Mbps VPN gateway with 5 SSL connections in Beijing with the bill-by-traffic option and uses a total of 5 GB traffic between 07:00:00-07:59:59, then at 08:00:00, the user will be required to pay the following fees:
+ Traffic fee: The traffic price is 0.12 USD/GB in Beijing. Traffic fee = traffic price × used traffic = 0.12 USD/GB × 5 GB = 0.6 USD
+ Gateway fee: The price of 50 Mbps VPN gateway in Beijing region is 0.074 USD/hr. Gateway fee = gateway price × duration = 0.074 USD/hr × 1 hour = 0.074 USD
+ SSL connection fee: The price of an SSL connection in Beijing is 0.003 USD/hr. SSL connection fee = SSL connection price × number of SSL connections × duration = 0.003 USD/hr × 5 × 1 hour = 0.015 USD

Total fee = traffic fee + gateway fee + SSL connection fee = 0.6 USD + 0.074 USD + 0.015 USD = 0.689 USD
