1. Log in to the [VPC Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/vpc).
2. Choose **IP and ENI** -> **ENI** in the left sidebar to go to the ENI list page.
3. Locate the ENI you want to unbind from a CVM,and click **Unbind CVM**under its **Operation** column.
 ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/c7680cb41b393e6cedba3d48ae2ee803.png)
4. Click **OK** in the pop-up window.
>When an ENI is unbound from a CVM, its associated private IPs, EIPs, and security groups are retained.
