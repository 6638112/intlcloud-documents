## Feature Description
Operation logs record the operations you performed on clusters in the EMR console, such as creating and scaling clusters.

## Directions
After successfully creating a cluster, log in to the [EMR console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/emr), **select a region in the top-left corner of the cluster list**, click the **ID/Name** of the target cluster to enter the cluster details page, and click **Operation Logs** on the left.
