## Feature Overview
An operation log records the operations that you perform on clusters in the EMR Console, such as creating and scaling clusters, for easy operation tracking.

## Directions
After you successfully create a cluster, log in to the [EMR Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/emr), select **Cluster List**, and click the ID/Name of the target cluster to enter the cluster details page. In the left sidebar, click **Operation Logs**.
>You can switch the region in the top-left corner in the **Cluster List**.
