From seven days before your resource expires until it is released, the system will send alerts to your Tencent Cloud account creator and collaborators as configured in [message subscription](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/message/user) by email and SMS.


| **Billing Mode** | **Isolation and Release Policy**                                           |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Pay-as-you-go     | If your Tencent Cloud account has overdue payments for two hours, all postpaid resources under your account will be isolated and become unavailable; after 24 hours of isolation, the resources and data will be released and deleted, and the deleted data cannot be recovered. |
