## Overview
When you don't need a record any longer, you can delete it. Once deleted, the record will stop taking effect.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [Private DNS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/privatedns) and click **Private Domain List** on the left sidebar to enter the private domain list.
2. In the **Private Domain List**, select the domain for which you want to delete a record or click **DNS** in the **Operation** column to enter its **DNS Records** page as shown below:
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/abbd4c5f5efd47d5e4eec4f62b852780.png)
3. Select the record you need to delete and click **Delete** in the **Operation** column to delete it as shown below:
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/bf0a4009f32c4c0b9a79b619e28860c9.png)
>!For private domains that are associated with VPCs, the VPCs should be disassociated first before you can delete records for them.
