## Overview
This document describes how to delete a private domain.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [Private DNS console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/privatedns) and click **Private Domain List** on the left sidebar to enter the private domain list.
2. In the **Private Domain List**, select the private domain to be deleted and click **More** > **Delete** as shown below:
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/93a9b3ad46204c3cf0bbdbb1b5322a09.png)
3. In the **Delete Private Domain** pop-up window, click **Delete** as shown below:
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/ad90d46dc30cb7916576ed6b30b39f1b.png)
>?
>- Note that deleting a private domain will also delete all its DNS records.
>- When you delete a private domain that is associated with a VPC, you need to disassociate it from the VPC first before you can delete it.
