## Product Introduction
Anti-DDoS Advanced is a paid product provided for games, finance sector and websites to protect them from suffering from service unavailability caused by high-traffic DDoS attacks. This product directs attack traffic to the configured protective IPs for cleansing to ensure the stability and availability of real servers' services.
Anti-DDoS Advanced is accessed through public network proxy, and supports TCP, UDP, HTTP, HTTPS and HTTP2 protocols. It is suitable for such use cases as finance, e-commerce, and games.

## Key Features
### Multi-dimensional protection
|Protection Type       | Description                                              |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Malformed packet filtering | Filters frag flood, smurf, stream flood and land flood attacks, and IP, TCP and UDP malformed packets |
| Anti-DDoS at network layer | Filters UDP Flood, SYN Flood, TCP Flood, ICMP Flood, ACK Flood, FIN Flood, RST Flood and DNS/NTP/SSDP reflection attacks and null sessions. |
| Anti-DDoS at application layer | Filters CC attacks and HTTP slow attacks, and supports HTTP custom filtering such as host filtering, user-agent filtering and referer filtering. |

### Elastic protection
You can adjust DDoS protection bandwidth flexibly and upgrade to a higher level of protection at any time to ensure uninterrupted services.

### Precise protection statistics
The presentation of multi-dimensional statistics of DDoS attacks, CC attacks, forwarded traffic and other data allows you to keep track of services and attacks in real time.

## Supported Regions
Anti-DDoS Advanced can protect all kinds of servers connected to internet, including but not limited to customer IDCs, Tencent Cloud and other clouds. Anti-DDoS Advanced is now available in following regions:
- Mainland China: South China (Guangzhou), East China (Shanghai), North China (Beijing).
- Outside Mainland China: Hong Kong, Asia-Pacific (Singapore, Seoul, Bangkok, India, Japan), Western U.S. (Silicon Valley), Eastern U.S. (Virginia), North America (Toronto), Europe (Frankfurt, Moscow).
