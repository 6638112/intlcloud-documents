1. Log in to the [Tencent Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/) and select **Products** > **Cloud Compute & Network** > **Virtual Private Cloud** to enter the VPC Console.
2. Click **Route Tables** in the left pane.
3. Filter the regions and VPCs at the top of the list.
4. Click the ID of the routing table to be viewed to enter the details page where you can see the information of routing policies whose next hop is CCN.
