1. Log in to the [Tencent Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/) and select **Products** > **Cloud Compute & Network** > **Virtual Private Cloud** to enter the VPC Console.
2. Click **Direct Connect Gateway** in the left pane.
3. In the list, click the newly created CCN-type Direct Connect gateway to enter the details page.
4. Click the **IDC IP address range** tab, enter the IDC range to connect to and save.
