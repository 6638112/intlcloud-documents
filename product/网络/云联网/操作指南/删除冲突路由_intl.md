1. Log in to the [Tencent Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/) and select **Products** > **Cloud Compute & Network** > **Virtual Private Cloud** to enter the VPC Console.
2. Click **Routing Tables** in the left pane.
3. In the list, click the ID of the routing table to be deleted to enter the details page.
4. In the list, find the row of the routing policy to be deleted and click **Delete** in the **Operation** column.
5. Click **OK**.
