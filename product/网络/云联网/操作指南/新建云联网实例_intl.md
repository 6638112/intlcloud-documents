1. Log in to the [Tencent Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/) and select **Products** > **Cloud Compute & Network** > **Virtual Private Cloud** to enter the VPC Console.
2. Click **Cloud Connect Network** in the left pane to enter the CCN management page.
3. Click **New**. 
 ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/4189c3d3af70c389a81159a12198a21c.png)
4. Enter the CCN instance name and remarks in the pop-up box.
5. Associate a network instance (you can also first create one and then associate it).
For example, for subnet A (192.168.1.0/24) in VPC1 in Guangzhou, select **VPC**, **South China (Guangzhou)** and the corresponding VPC.
 ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/08633b1dfb6c4f4eb3b7b0713e5c0fa5.png)
6. Click **OK** to create the CCN instance.
