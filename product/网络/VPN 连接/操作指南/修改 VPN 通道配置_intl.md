1. Log in to [Tencent Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/), and click **Products** -> **Virtual Private Cloud** to go to the VPC console.
2. Click **VPN Connection** -> **VPN Tunnel** in the left pane to go to the management page.
3. Click the ID of the VPN tunnel to be modified to go to the details page.
4. In the basic information page, modify the basic information and SPD policy.
 ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/59d30ce72604d8ee390764957897507a.png)
5. Click the **Advanced Configuration** tab to modify IKE and IPsec configurations.
 ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/a34fee630f81a79f9616300223bf8b6f.png)
