After creating an NAT gateway, you can manage the EIPs of the gateway by following the steps below.
1. Log in to [Tencent Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/), select **Products** -> **Virtual Private Cloud** to go to the VPC console, and then click **NAT Gateway** on the left pane.
2. In the list page, select the NAT gateway you want to work with to go to its details page.
3. Click **Associate EIP**, and then choose to bind or unbind an EIP on this page.
 - Binding an EIP
 Click **Bind EIP**, choose the EIP to be bound in the drop-down box, and then click **Save**.
 ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/5b503b63af709be21630c4d32be8c0c6.png)
 - Unbinding EIP
 Click **Unbind** in the **Operation** column for the EIP to be unbound.
 >**Note:**
 > Be sure to retain at least one EIP.
 
 ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/67f6a8da57017f87407a5aba06c31638.png)
