### What's the price of a domain name?
The purchase, transfer and renewal prices vary with domain names. For the latest prices, see the Tencent Cloud official website.

### Is there a special offer if I purchase a domain name for multiple years?
Tencent Cloud does not provide special offers for the purchase of a domain name for multiple years, but provide one for the purchase of a domain name in the first year.

### Is there a special offer if I register domain names in batch?
We provide special offers based on the number of domain names you registered. You can [submit a ticket](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/workorder/category/create?level1_id=16&level2_id=17&level1_name=%E5%85%B6%E4%BB%96%E6%9C%8D%E5%8A%A1&level2_name=%E5%9F%9F%E5%90%8D) to inquire about the detailed prices.

### Will domain name prices change in the future?
The prices of domain names (including prices for purchase, renewal and transfer) may be adjusted according to market conditions. You should follow the prices published on the official website when purchasing domain names.
